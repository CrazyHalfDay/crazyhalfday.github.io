Phengite-mediated fluorine and chlorine fluxes from subduction zones to the deep mantle

由金云母介导的从俯冲带到深部地幔的氟和氯通量

2026-04-01
Science Advances (IF: 11.7)

Yingzhuo Liu，Yu Wang，Chunfei Chen，Mingdi Gao，Yi-Gang Xu

DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.aee6110

The cycling of volatiles through subduction zones fundamentally shapes Earth’s chemical evolution, yet how fluorine (F) and chlorine (Cl) are processed in the deep mantle remains enigmatic. Here, we use high-pressure experiments (5 to 11 gigapascals and 850° to 1200°C) on altered oceanic crust analogs to track deep halogen behavior. We show that phengite remains stable to 11 gigapascals and 1050°C, efficiently transporting F and Cl to ~330-kilometer depth. Upon breakdown or melting, phengite strongly fractionates these halogens: Cl is released into fluids or melts, while F is sequestered in residual phases such as KMgF 3 . The Cl-rich fluids produced (9.6 to 19.9 wt % Cl) closely match high-density fluid inclusions in diamonds, implicating them as key agents of cratonic metasomatism. Our findings establish phengite as a critical carrier mediating postarc fluxes of F (1.7 × 10 12 to 2.6 × 10 12 grams per year) and Cl (0.52 × 10 12 to 1.1 × 10 12 grams per year), providing a major pathway for replenishing deep mantle halogen reservoirs.

研究背景：俯冲带是地球挥发分循环的核心通道，决定了卤素等元素如何在地表、地壳和地幔之间迁移，并深刻影响地球长期化学演化。然而，相比水和碳，氟（F）与氯（Cl）在深部俯冲过程中如何被保存、分异并输送到深部地幔，长期缺乏直接实验约束。该研究的动机在于识别俯冲洋壳中能够稳定携带卤素进入深部地幔的关键矿物相，并量化其对深部地幔卤素补给和克拉通交代作用的贡献。

方法详情：作者采用高压高温实验方法，使用蚀变洋壳（altered oceanic crust）模拟体系，在5至11 GPa、850至1200°C的条件下系统追踪F和Cl在俯冲环境中的分配行为，这一压力温度范围对应俯冲板片进入上地幔深部至约330公里深度的条件。实验重点考察了富钾白云母高压变种——phengite（多硅白云母/硅铁白云母）在不同温压条件下的稳定性，以及其在分解或发生部分熔融时卤素在矿物、熔体和流体之间的再分配。研究还识别了残留相中可能固定F的矿物，如KMgF3，并测定或估算了流体/熔体中的Cl富集程度。最后，作者将实验结果与天然金刚石中的高密度流体包裹体成分进行对比，并结合俯冲通量估算，推导phengite介导的深部F、Cl后弧输送通量。

研究结果：研究表明，phengite在高达11 GPa和1050°C的条件下仍可保持稳定，说明它能够在俯冲过程中高效携带F和Cl到约330公里深度，是深部卤素输运的重要载体。当phengite分解或体系发生熔融时，F和Cl表现出显著分异：Cl优先进入流体或熔体，而F则更倾向于保留在残留固相中，例如KMgF3等含氟相。实验产生的Cl富流体含有9.6至19.9 wt%的Cl，与金刚石高密度流体包裹体的成分高度相似，这支持了此类流体可能是克拉通地幔交代作用的重要介质。基于这些结果，作者进一步估算phengite控制的后弧深部通量约为F的1.7×10^12至2.6×10^12克/年、Cl的0.52×10^12至1.1×10^12克/年，表明该机制可能是补给深部地幔卤素储库的主要途径之一。

局限性：首先，该研究主要基于实验室模拟的蚀变洋壳类比体系，天然俯冲板片在成分、氧逸度、含水量和变质历史上的复杂性，可能导致实际卤素行为比实验结果更具空间和时间上的非均一性。其次，实验虽覆盖了关键的上地幔深部条件，但对更深部条件、不同俯冲热结构以及与其他含卤矿物共同作用的约束仍然有限。另一个潜在问题是全球通量估算依赖若干参数假设，例如俯冲输入组成和矿物稳定域范围，因此数值上仍存在一定不确定性。

未来方向：后续研究可进一步扩展到更高压温条件，以检验F和Cl在过渡带乃至更深地幔中的持续迁移与储存机制。与此同时，结合天然样品地球化学、流体包裹体分析和热力学建模，将有助于更精确评估不同类型俯冲带中的全球卤素循环通量。

Progressive sub-arc mantle oxidation modulated by sediment melt

沉积物熔体调控的弧下地幔渐进氧化

2026-04-03
Science Advances (IF: 11.7)

Mingdi Gao，Yu Wang，Yi-Gang Xu

DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.aeb9023

The redox state of the sub-arc mantle beneath volcanic arcs is typically oxidized but exhibits substantial variability. Previous studies proposed that the incorporation of slab components alters both arc magma compositions and sub-arc mantle redox state, whereas the dominant component remains debated. Here, by compiling global Cenozoic primitive arc basalt and olivine-hosted melt inclusion data, we show that sediment melt dominated by terrigenous origin plays the controlling role. Progressive influx of sediment melts into the sub-arc mantle results in gradual enrichment of potassium oxide and most incompatible elements in arc magmas. Increases in potassium oxide content in primitive magmas also correlates with rising oxygen fugacity ( f o 2 ) values, highlighting the strong oxidizing potential of sediment melt. The high–potassium oxide, high- f o 2 arc magmas spatially correlate with the distribution of Cenozoic porphyry copper-gold deposits, showing that the sediment melt influx not only elevates the sub-arc mantle f o 2 but also enhances the metallogenetic potential for porphyry deposits.

研究背景：俯冲带火山弧下方地幔楔通常表现为相对氧化的氧逸度状态，但不同弧段之间存在显著差异，而这种氧化性的来源及其主控因素长期存在争议。既有研究普遍认为，俯冲板片释放的流体或熔体会改变弧岩浆组成并影响地幔氧化还原状态，但究竟是脱水流体、蚀变洋壳熔体还是沉积物熔体起主导作用并不明确。该研究的动机在于利用全球尺度的新生代原始弧玄武岩及橄榄石包裹熔体数据，识别控制弧下地幔氧化和岩浆富集特征的关键板片组分，并进一步评估其与斑岩铜金成矿之间的联系。

Huang Botao，Sun Jiming，Sui Qinglin，Wang Guoqiang，Luo Keyong 等

DOI: 10.1111/1755-6724.70039

ABSTRACT The prolonged subduction history of the Palaeo‐Asian Ocean remains pivotal to reconstructing the accretion history of the Central Asian Orogenic Belt, yet its duration and tectonic evolution are debated due to ambiguous ophiolite records. Here, we identified Early Cambrian and Early Permian gabbros in the Jijitaizi ophiolite of the Beishan orogen, obtaining zircon U‐Pb ages of 514 ± 3, 292 ± 3 and 289 ± 2 Ma. Early Cambrian gabbros exhibit back‐arc basin affinities: N‐MORB‐like REE patterns (∑REE = 22.55–30.11 ppm) with subduction‐fluid imprints (Th/Ta > 1 and negative Nb anomalies), indicating derivation from depleted MORB mantle metasomatised by slab fluids. Early Permian gabbros show ultra‐depleted forearc cumulate signatures: extreme REE depletion (∑REE = 3.48–4.81 ppm), high ε Nd ( t ) (+10.49 to +14.07), low Ti/V (4.62–6.08), high Ca # and anorthitic plagioclase (An 80–93 ). The regional data collectively constrain the Jijitaizi–Xiaohuangshan and Hongliuhe–Xichangjing ophiolites as a unified back‐arc system in the Early Cambrian, also providing evidence for long‐term subduction from Early Cambrian to Early Permian.

Apatite geochemistry and Sr isotopes reveal the genesis of Au–W deposits in the Xiangzhong Metallogenic Province, South China: Insights from the Xingfengshan deposit

磷灰石地球化学与 Sr 同位素揭示华南湘中成矿省 Au–W 矿床的成因：以兴丰山矿床为例

2026-03-30
Journal of Asian Earth Sciences (IF: 2.4)

Lianjie Zhao，Yu Zhang，Han Zheng，Hao Zeng，Hongjie Shen 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.jseaes.2026.107074

Metasedimentary rock-hosted Au–W deposits constitute a globally significant resource for gold and tungsten, yet their genesis remains debated, primarily due to uncertainties in linking mineralization to regional magmatism. The Xiangzhong Metallogenic Province (XZMP) represents a world-class Au–W metallogenic domain, providing a valuable window to address this issue. We conducted an integrated study of apatite from the Xingfengshan deposit, combining textural analysis with trace element and Sr isotopic measurements. Two types of apatite were identified: Ap-1 from skarn W ore and Ap-2 from auriferous sheeted quartz veins. Both types exhibit homogeneous textures and middle REE-enriched patterns. Nevertheless, they differ significantly in Eu anomalies: Ap-1 exhibits negative anomalies (Eu/Eu* = 0.5–0.9), whereas Ap-2 shows positive anomalies (Eu/Eu* = 1.9–3.7). Integrated with existing geochronological and petrogenetic analyses, REE patterns and Sr isotope ratios indicate that Ap-1 formed via interaction between magmatic-hydrothermal fluid from the Longcangwan granite and the Neoproterozoic Mobin Formation calcareous-interlayered slate. In contrast, Ap-2 likely formed from the interaction between magmatic-hydrothermal fluid corresponding to the Longtan granite and the Neoproterozoic Gaojian Formation slate. Isotopic modeling further reveals significantly stronger fluid-rock interaction during W mineralization (fluid/rock mass ratio = 10.0–34.7) compared to Au mineralization (fluid/rock mass ratio = 0.6–0.8). This study not only establishes genetic links between Au–W mineralization and specific magmatism, supporting an intrusion-related origin for the Au–W deposits in XZMP, but also highlights apatite as a powerful geochemical tracer for reconstructing the evolution of hydrothermal fluid in metasedimentary-hosted Au–W systems.
