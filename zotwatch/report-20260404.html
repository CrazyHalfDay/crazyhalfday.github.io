Phengite-mediated fluorine and chlorine fluxes from subduction zones to the deep mantle

由金云母介导的从俯冲带到深部地幔的氟和氯通量

2026-04-01
Science Advances (IF: 11.7)

Yingzhuo Liu，Yu Wang，Chunfei Chen，Mingdi Gao，Yi-Gang Xu

DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.aee6110

The cycling of volatiles through subduction zones fundamentally shapes Earth’s chemical evolution, yet how fluorine (F) and chlorine (Cl) are processed in the deep mantle remains enigmatic. Here, we use high-pressure experiments (5 to 11 gigapascals and 850° to 1200°C) on altered oceanic crust analogs to track deep halogen behavior. We show that phengite remains stable to 11 gigapascals and 1050°C, efficiently transporting F and Cl to ~330-kilometer depth. Upon breakdown or melting, phengite strongly fractionates these halogens: Cl is released into fluids or melts, while F is sequestered in residual phases such as KMgF 3 . The Cl-rich fluids produced (9.6 to 19.9 wt % Cl) closely match high-density fluid inclusions in diamonds, implicating them as key agents of cratonic metasomatism. Our findings establish phengite as a critical carrier mediating postarc fluxes of F (1.7 × 10 12 to 2.6 × 10 12 grams per year) and Cl (0.52 × 10 12 to 1.1 × 10 12 grams per year), providing a major pathway for replenishing deep mantle halogen reservoirs.

研究背景：俯冲带是地球挥发分循环的核心通道，决定了卤素等元素如何在地表、地壳和地幔之间迁移，并深刻影响地球长期化学演化。然而，相比水和碳，氟（F）与氯（Cl）在深部俯冲过程中如何被保存、分异并输送到深部地幔，长期缺乏直接实验约束。该研究的动机在于识别俯冲洋壳中能够稳定携带卤素进入深部地幔的关键矿物相，并量化其对深部地幔卤素补给和克拉通交代作用的贡献。

方法详情：作者采用高压高温实验方法，使用蚀变洋壳（altered oceanic crust）模拟体系，在5至11 GPa、850至1200°C的条件下系统追踪F和Cl在俯冲环境中的分配行为，这一压力温度范围对应俯冲板片进入上地幔深部至约330公里深度的条件。实验重点考察了富钾白云母高压变种——phengite（多硅白云母/硅铁白云母）在不同温压条件下的稳定性，以及其在分解或发生部分熔融时卤素在矿物、熔体和流体之间的再分配。研究还识别了残留相中可能固定F的矿物，如KMgF3，并测定或估算了流体/熔体中的Cl富集程度。最后，作者将实验结果与天然金刚石中的高密度流体包裹体成分进行对比，并结合俯冲通量估算，推导phengite介导的深部F、Cl后弧输送通量。

研究结果：研究表明，phengite在高达11 GPa和1050°C的条件下仍可保持稳定，说明它能够在俯冲过程中高效携带F和Cl到约330公里深度，是深部卤素输运的重要载体。当phengite分解或体系发生熔融时，F和Cl表现出显著分异：Cl优先进入流体或熔体，而F则更倾向于保留在残留固相中，例如KMgF3等含氟相。实验产生的Cl富流体含有9.6至19.9 wt%的Cl，与金刚石高密度流体包裹体的成分高度相似，这支持了此类流体可能是克拉通地幔交代作用的重要介质。基于这些结果，作者进一步估算phengite控制的后弧深部通量约为F的1.7×10^12至2.6×10^12克/年、Cl的0.52×10^12至1.1×10^12克/年，表明该机制可能是补给深部地幔卤素储库的主要途径之一。

局限性：首先，该研究主要基于实验室模拟的蚀变洋壳类比体系，天然俯冲板片在成分、氧逸度、含水量和变质历史上的复杂性，可能导致实际卤素行为比实验结果更具空间和时间上的非均一性。其次，实验虽覆盖了关键的上地幔深部条件，但对更深部条件、不同俯冲热结构以及与其他含卤矿物共同作用的约束仍然有限。另一个潜在问题是全球通量估算依赖若干参数假设，例如俯冲输入组成和矿物稳定域范围，因此数值上仍存在一定不确定性。

未来方向：后续研究可进一步扩展到更高压温条件，以检验F和Cl在过渡带乃至更深地幔中的持续迁移与储存机制。与此同时，结合天然样品地球化学、流体包裹体分析和热力学建模，将有助于更精确评估不同类型俯冲带中的全球卤素循环通量。

Tin isotope fractionation in arc magmas controlled by degassing and slab input

弧岩浆中锡同位素分馏受脱气作用和板片输入控制

2026-03-31
Geochimica et Cosmochimica Acta (IF: 4.5)

Wei-Cheng Jiang，Jia-Xin She，Alexandra Davidson，Chun-Fei Chen，Chris Firth 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.gca.2026.03.047

As a redox-sensitive and volatile-chalcophile tracer, Sn isotopes provide unique insights into the origin and transport mechanisms of Sn at convergent margins, yet the drivers of their fractionation remains poorly understood due to a lack of integrated spatial and process-oriented studies. We present high-precision Sn isotopic compositions of lavas (pumices) and hydrothermal products and clinopyroxene-inferred H 2 O concentrations from Whakaari (arc-front) and Taranaki (rear-arc) in the Kermadec- Hikurangi subduction system.

研究问题：探讨俯冲带弧岩浆中锡同位素分馏受脱气与板片输入如何共同控制。

研究方法：测定Kermadec-Hikurangi两火山浮岩与热液产物高精度锡同位素，并用单斜辉石反演H2O。

主要发现：前弧与后弧锡同位素差异表明，岩浆脱气和板片流体输入共同主导锡迁移与分馏。

创新点：整合空间对比、过程示踪及水含量约束，系统揭示俯冲带锡同位素分馏机制。

相关性：为理解俯冲带挥发分循环、氧化还原演化及锡成矿物质来源提供关键地球化学约束。

研究背景：锡（Sn）是一种对氧化还原条件敏感、同时具有挥发性和亲硫性的元素，因此其同位素体系有潜力追踪俯冲带中挥发分迁移、板片输入以及岩浆去气过程。尽管Sn同位素被认为能够为会聚板块边缘的物质来源与迁移机制提供独特约束，但其分馏驱动因素长期缺乏系统认识，尤其缺少同时结合空间对比（弧前—弧后）与过程约束（岩浆—热液）的综合研究。该研究以Kermadec-Hikurangi俯冲系统中的Whakaari（弧前）与Taranaki（弧后）为对象，旨在识别Sn同位素变化究竟主要受去气作用、板片组分输入还是二者共同控制。

方法详情：作者对Whakaari和Taranaki的火山熔岩/浮岩样品以及相关热液产物进行了高精度Sn同位素测定，以建立不同构造位置与不同地球化学过程下的Sn同位素变化特征。研究同时利用单斜辉石反演的岩浆H2O含量，作为约束岩浆含水性、挥发分行为和去气强度的重要参数，从而将Sn同位素分馏与岩浆挥发过程联系起来。通过比较弧前与弧后岩浆体系、喷出物与热液产物之间的差异，研究试图区分深部板片贡献与浅部火山—热液过程对Sn同位素的相对影响。整体方法属于同位素地球化学与岩石学/挥发分约束相结合的过程导向研究框架，强调空间对比与机制判别。

研究结果：研究的核心结果表明，弧岩浆中的Sn同位素分馏并非单一来源控制，而是与岩浆去气和板片输入密切相关，二者共同塑造了Kermadec-Hikurangi俯冲系统中的Sn同位素特征。弧前Whakaari与弧后Taranaki之间的差异，结合单斜辉石反演的H2O信息，说明含水量与挥发分释放程度会影响Sn在岩浆—气相/热液相之间的分配与同位素分馏。热液产物与火山喷出物之间的对比进一步支持Sn在浅部脱气—热液活动中的迁移和再分配作用。其意义在于，Sn同位素不仅可作为俯冲带板片组分输入的示踪剂，也可作为识别岩浆去气、挥发分演化以及潜在金属成矿过程的重要工具。

The molybdenum isotope composition of the Bulk Silicate Earth constrained from molybdenites and its implications for the nature of late-stage building blocks

2026-03-29
Earth and Planetary Science Letters (IF: 4.8)

Yao Sun，Jonas Tusch，Xiaorui Fan，Jifeng Xu，Chao Li 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.epsl.2026.120012

The mass-independent Mo isotope composition of the Bulk Silicate Earth (BSE) bears great potential to investigate the origin of the Earth’s latest 10–20% planetary building blocks. However, currently different estimates for the Mo isotope composition of the BSE render constraints on the composition of late-stage accretionary materials difficult. To address this issue and to revisit the Mo isotope composition of the BSE, we report high-precision molybdenum isotope data for a comprehensive set of terrestrial molybdenites from different locations around the globe covering mineralization ages that extend from the Archean to the Phanerozoic. The molybdenite results are used to constrain the Mo isotope composition of the BSE as follows: ε 92 Mo = 0.04 ± 0.06, ε 94 Mo = 0.03 ± 0.03, ε 95 Mo = 0.01 ± 0.01, ε 97 Mo = 0.02 ± 0.02, ε 100 Mo = 0.05 ± 0.06 ( n = 16, 95% confidence interval, relative to the NIST SRM 3134 Mo standard). In contrast to previous studies, no resolvable ε 94 Mo and ε 95 Mo anomalies were observed, suggesting a BSE composition with predominantly non-carbonaceous chondrite provenance. Considering the analytical uncertainties of our new BSE estimate and literature data for carbonaceous and non-carbonaceous meteorites, it remains a viable option that 12±10% of the present-day Mo budget in the BSE derives from carbonaceous meteorite material delivered during late-stage accretion. This amount of Mo is consistent with the fraction of Mo that was delivered to Earth during its final 0.5% of accretion by the late veneer.

Paleoarchean continental emergence in the North China Craton indicated by low δ18O granites

Chao Wang，Hang Chu，Shuguang Song，Guochun Zhao，Mark B. Allen 等

DOI: 10.1130/b38794.1

Emergent continental crust is important for the evolution of Earth’s surface system and the development of habitability, and it is apparent that early Archean cratons were locally emerged. However, it remains unresolved if the North China Craton, which preserves ancient (4.1−4.0 Ga) crustal remnants, had emergent continental crust during the early Archean. The identification of Paleoarchean TTG crust altered by meteoric water in Eastern Hebei indicates the emergence of continental crust in the North China Craton during 3.6−3.2 Ga, and this Paleoarchean continental emergence could have been associated with magmatic underplating during mantle plume activities, which is also evidenced by the Paleoarchean enriched plume remnants in Eastern Hebei.

Abstract The Cambrian-Devonian platform carbonate rocks found throughout the eastern Canadian Cordillera are important hosts of Zn-Pb sulfide mineralization. Models for mineralization commonly invoke either Laramide compression, or hydrothermal circulation associated with an active tectonic margin that was present during the Paleozoic. To distinguish between these possibilities, we have dated two Zn-Pb deposits from the northeastern Canadian Cordillera (Robb Lake and Prairie Creek) using pyrite Re-Os geochronology. The age of Robb Lake (348 ± 26 Ma) overlaps with that of the clastic-dominated deposits in the Kechika trough, located directly to the west, and is consistent with both sets of deposit types being related to a common metallogenic system. Pyrite collected from stratabound sulfides and quartz-carbonate-sulfide veins from the main orebody at Prairie Creek was dated to 315.7 ± 8.3 Ma and is statistically indistinguishable from the age of 322 ± 10 Ma calculated from Mississippi Valley-type mineralization distal to the main orebody. Our Re-Os dates require that mineralization at Prairie Creek and Robb Lake occurred during the Paleozoic, in stark contrast to previous age estimates made by paleomagnetism (&lt;150 Ma). Consequently, Zn-Pb mineralization in the northern cordillera formed in response to an active margin that existed along the western margin of North America during the Paleozoic, and it is not related to the Laramide compression.
