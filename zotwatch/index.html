Magnetotelluric Imaging of a Magma Plumbing System From the Asthenosphere to Crust Beneath the Newer Volcanic Province, Australia

澳大利亚新生代火山区从软流圈到地壳的岩浆通道系统的大地电磁成像

2025-12-26
Geophysical Research Letters (IF: 4.6)

Xinpeng Ma，Yunhe Liu，Changchun Yin，Haibin Yang，Zhihao Rong 等

DOI: 10.1029/2025gl118189

Abstract The Newer Volcanic Province (NVP) in southeastern Australia is a classic example of continental intraplate volcanism located far from a plate boundary, yet the configuration of its magma plumbing system remains unclear. Using three‐dimensional (3D) magnetotelluric (MT) inversion, we imaged a vertically continuous, trans‐lithospheric conductive structure beneath the NVP. This structure consists of a sub‐horizontal zone in the asthenospheric mantle, interpreted as 0.1–1.0 vol.% carbonate‐rich melt, vertically linked to hydrous basaltic melt zones (2–5 vol.%) in the lithospheric mantle and shallow crust. These upper crust conductive zones likely represent remnant melt or metasomatic minerals. These features define a vertically integrated magma plumbing system from the asthenosphere to the surface. The geometry and depth of the anomalies are consistent with scenarios in which the carbonate‐rich melt may form in response to shear‐driven upwelling and edge‐driven convection. These observations provide new constraints on possible mantle processes that drive intraplate volcanism.

研究问题：厘清远离板块边界的澳大利亚Newer Volcanic Province岩浆通道系统结构

研究方法：三维大地电磁反演获取地壳-地幔电阻率模型

主要发现：发现从软流圈到地壳的直立高导体，解释为含碳酸盐熔体与含水玄武质熔体网络

创新点：首次用MT揭示板内火山跨岩石圈完整岩浆通道三维几何

相关性：为板内火山成因机制与深部碳循环研究提供地球物理约束

研究背景：The Newer Volcanic Province (NVP) is a textbook case of continental intraplate volcanism far from active plate boundaries, yet its deep magma supply network has remained enigmatic. Understanding how melt is generated and transported through >100 km of lithosphere is essential for deciphering the engine of such isolated volcanic activity.

方法详情：The authors collected a dense array of broadband and long-period magnetotelluric (MT) stations across the NVP and performed rigorous 3-D inversion to recover electrical resistivity from the surface to ~250 km depth. Anisotropic and topography-cognizant forward modelling were incorporated to minimise artefacts, while iterative sensitivity tests constrained the minimum melt fraction required to explain observed conductors. The final resistivity model was jointly interpreted with published seismic tomography and geochemical data to discriminate melt from metasomatic phases.

研究结果：A vertically continuous, sub-vertical low-resistivity anomaly (<10 Ωm) links a carbonate-rich asthenospheric melt layer (0.1–1.0 vol.%) at ~110 km to hydrous basaltic reservoirs (2–5 vol.%) in the lithospheric mantle and upper crust beneath the NVP. The geometry supports shear-driven upwelling and edge-driven convection as viable mechanisms for intraplate melt generation, and implies that the NVP is fed by a trans-lithospheric magma plumbing system rather than by isolated pockets. These findings provide the first geophysical image of a fully connected melt pathway from asthenosphere to surface in southeastern Australia.

局限性：MT resolution decreases with depth, so the exact thickness and lateral continuity of the carbonate-rich layer remain uncertain. The inferred melt fractions depend on assumed carbonatite–basalt resistivity relationships, which can vary with volatile content and temperature; trade-offs with graphite or sulfides are not uniquely ruled out.

未来方向：Higher-resolution ocean-bottom MT and joint inversion with seismic anisotropy could sharpen the deep mantle flow pattern, while targeted noble-gas geochemistry of the youngest NVP cones may test the carbonate-rich source hypothesis.

研究相关性：Researchers investigating intraplate volcanism, lithosphere–asthenosphere interaction, or electrical-resistivity signatures of mantle melts will find this paper a key reference for imaging methodologies and for comparative studies in other continental settings.

Water Incorporation Mechanisms and Effects in MgSiO <sub>3</sub> ‐Majorite Under High Temperature and Pressure Conditions

高温高压条件下MgSiO₃-Majorite中水的赋存机制与效应

2025-12-27
Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth (IF: 3.9)

Yancheng Lou，Zhigang Zhang，Andrew M. Walker，Stephen Stackhouse，Meng Chen 等

DOI: 10.1029/2025jb032448

Abstract The incorporation of water in high‐pressure minerals is essential for the water cycle within the interiors of terrestrial planets. Majoritic garnet, a major component in the mantles of Earth and Mars, plays a significant role in this context. In this study, we use first‐principles simulations to explore water incorporation mechanisms in MgSiO 3 ‐majorite, which is a key end‐member of majoritic garnet, at conditions up to 2,000 K and 20 GPa. By dealing with the relationship between chemical potential and the Gibbs free energy changes for the reactions at equilibrium conditions, we determine the ratios of the seven potential hydrous defects. Our results reveal that the Si2 and Si3 defects, which are of the hydrogarnet‐type, dominate water incorporation in MgSiO 3 ‐majorite. In addition, we evaluate the effects of these hydrous defects on seismic wave velocities. The presence of Si2 and Si3 defects, with an expected water concentration of ∼700 ppm, has a small effect on both P‐wave and S‐wave velocities. Nevertheless, the influence of water on lateral variations in the seismic wave velocities of MgSiO 3 ‐majorite, which is opposite to that found for ringwoodite, offers a potential tool for investigating compositional heterogeneities in hydrated regions of planetary mantles.

方法详情：作者采用基于密度泛函理论的第一性原理分子动力学，在0–20 GPa、300–2000 K范围内计算七种潜在含氢缺陷的形成焓与振动熵；通过构建缺陷反应并与外界水化学势平衡，获得各缺陷在热力学平衡下的浓度比。进一步利用有限应变弹性理论，计算含约700 ppm水的样品相对于无水样品的P、S波速变化。

局限性：计算限于MgSiO₃端元，未考虑Fe、Al、Na等天然石榴石组分对缺陷能的调控；20 GPa以下结果可能低估更深部更高压下的缺陷稳定性；700 ppm为平衡预测，实际淬火样品可能受动力学因素偏离该值。

Seismic Anisotropy and Mantle Deformation Beneath Northeast China: Insight From Shear Wave Splitting Analysis

中国东北地震各向异性与地幔变形：剪切波分裂分析的启示

2025-12-27
Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth (IF: 3.9)

Zhengyang Qiang，Qingju Wu，Yonghua Li

DOI: 10.1029/2025jb031814

Abstract This study investigates upper mantle deformation beneath northeast (NE) China by reanalyzing shear wave splitting measurements from the WAVESArray network. We identify potential overestimations of anisotropy strength in previous studies, likely caused by sensor misorientation and misclassification of null measurements. Our analysis yields 110 well‐constrained splitting measurements from 66 stations and 43 high‐quality null measurements from 28 stations. The mean delay time is 0.82 ± 0.28 s, consistent with the global continental average (∼1.0 s) but significantly smaller than prior estimates. Based on the spatial coherence analysis of shear wave splitting parameters and previous P ‐wave anisotropy results, we conclude that the anisotropy mainly originates in the lithosphere or the upper mantle transition zone (MTZ), rather than the mantle wedge. Combining our measurements with previous shear wave splitting results, we infer that the predominant NNW‐SSE trending anisotropy reflects fossilized Mesozoic extensional deformation in the lithosphere or the influence of the Paleo‐Pacific Plate subduction within the MTZ. Additionally, in the easternmost Xing'an Block, localized ENE‐WSW fast directions may reflect preserved Proterozoic microcontinental fragments. The solely null measurements and weak anisotropy observed around the intraplate volcanoes suggest the possible presence of localized upwelling of hot material or oriented melt pockets. These findings highlight a complex interplay of multi‐scale mantle and lithospheric processes shaping seismic anisotropy in NE China, providing important insights into the geodynamic processes of the Central Asian Orogenic Belt.

主要发现：平均延迟0.82 s，各向异性源于岩石圈/MTZ而非地幔楔

相关性：为中亚造山带多尺度构造演化提供地幔变形新约束

研究背景：东北亚大陆下方上地幔的地震各向异性被广泛用于约束岩石圈变形和深部动力学过程，但早期基于WAVESArray的剪切波分裂结果给出的延迟时间普遍偏大，导致对岩石圈/软流圈变形强度及来源存在争议。作者怀疑这些高估源于台站方位角标定误差和null事件误分类，因此亟需系统重处理以获得更可靠的各向异性图像。

方法详情：作者重新挑选了WAVESArray 66个宽频带台站的远震SKS、SKKS和PKS震相，采用最小特征值算法在旋转相关法框架下测定快波偏振方向φ和延迟时间δt，并通过网格搜索+Bootstrap估计不确定度；对未出现可测分裂的事件，利用Bayesian空分类器识别43个高质量null结果，结合后方位角-入射角扫描排除台站方位角误差。随后对110个有效分裂和43个null进行空间一致性聚类，并与区域Pn各向异性、岩石圈厚度及MTZ速度异常对比，以区分岩石圈、地幔楔与MTZ的相对贡献。
