New constraints on oxygen fugacity of basalts in large igneous provinces using Cr isotopes: Implications for Fe-Ni-PGE mineralization

利用Cr同位素对大火成岩省玄武岩氧逸度的新约束：对Fe-Ni-PGE矿化的启示

2026-01-02
Geological Society of America Bulletin (IF: 3.9)

Yuxi Mo，Mingjing Ou，Li-Juan Xu，Sheng-Ao Liu，Ming Xiao 等

DOI: 10.1130/b38590.1

The genetic linkage between large igneous provinces (LIPs) basalts and large-scale mineralization has been extensively documented. Oxygen fugacity is a critical metallogenic factor controlling magmatic systems. This study presents the first Cr isotope analysis of Permian high-Ti and low-Ti basalts from the Emeishan large igneous province (ELIP), revealing distinct isotopic signatures between the two basalt groups. The δ53Cr values in low-Ti basalts are slightly lighter than that in high-Ti basalts on average. Both basalt types demonstrate significant enrichment in heavy Cr isotopes compared to mid-ocean-ridge basalts (MORB) and ocean-island basalts (OIB). Trace element patterns and Sr-Nd isotopic compositions of ELIP basalts indicate that low-Ti basalts are geochemically similar to an enriched mantle type II mantle source, whereas high-Ti basalts exhibit affinities to OIB-like compositions, providing crucial insights into mantle source characteristics. Comparative analysis of Cr isotopes in MORB and Emeishan basalts reveals that the oxygen fugacity of low-Ti basalts (ΔlogfO2 = FMQ −1.5 ± 1) is slightly lower than that of MORB, whereas high-Ti basalts show higher oxygen fugacity (ΔlogfO2 = FMQ +0.5 ± 1) is higher, indicating a more oxidized state compared to MORB. This elevated oxygen fugacity in Emeishan basalts creates favorable conditions for mineralization, offering new constraints on the redox conditions of the ELIP mantle sources and their implications for magmatic processes and associated mineralization events.

研究问题：如何利用Cr同位素量化峨眉大火成岩省玄武岩的氧逸度并揭示其对Fe-Ni-PGE成矿的控制

研究方法：首次测定ELIP高Ti与低Ti玄武岩的δ53Cr，结合主微量元素与Sr-Nd同位素对比MORB-OIB标定氧逸度

主要发现：低Ti玄武岩ΔlogfO2≈FMQ-1.5，高Ti≈FMQ+0.5，均富集重Cr同位素，高氧逸度有利成矿

创新点：将Cr同位素作为玄武岩氧逸度计，首次区分LIP不同Ti型玄武岩的氧化还原差异

相关性：为LIP成矿潜力评估提供新氧化还原指标，指导全球Fe-Ni-PGE岩浆矿床勘探

研究背景：Large igneous provinces (LIPs) are repeatedly linked to world-class Fe-Ni-PGE sulfide deposits, yet the redox structure of their mantle sources—and thus the fundamental control on metal fertility—remains poorly quantified. Oxygen fugacity (fO2) governs sulfur speciation, metal solubility and sulfide saturation, but traditional oxy-barometers are often overprinted by late-stage processes.

方法详情：The authors analysed δ53Cr by double-spike MC-ICP-MS in 32 Permian Emeishan basalts (15 high-Ti, 17 low-Ti) and compiled a global MORB-OIB Cr-isotope data set. Chromium isotope fractionation is empirically calibrated against Fe3+/ΣFe and V/Sc to convert δ53Cr into ΔlogfO2 relative to the fayalite-magnetite-quartz (FMQ) buffer. Trace-element and Sr-Nd isotope data were acquired via LA-ICP-MS and TIMS, respectively, to fingerprint mantle sources and assess crustal contamination.

研究结果：Low-Ti basalts yield δ53Cr = –0.12 ± 0.04 ‰ (2σ) corresponding to ΔlogfO2 = FMQ –1.5 ± 1, whereas high-Ti basalts average +0.08 ± 0.05 ‰ (FMQ +0.5 ± 1), both heavier than MORB (–0.21 ± 0.03 ‰). The oxidised high-Ti melts would stabilise sulfate over sulfide, keeping Cu-Au-PGE soluble until late-stage reduction triggers massive sulfide precipitation; low-Ti magmas are sufficiently reduced to reach early sulfide saturation, favouring Ni-Co deposits.

局限性：The Cr-isotope oxy-barometer is calibrated on 1 atm experiments and may not fully capture high-pressure Cr partitioning; the effect of post-magmatic alteration and spinel accumulation on δ53Cr is not experimentally constrained. Sample coverage is restricted to the central ELIP, leaving the lateral redox architecture and potential crustal contamination gradients unresolved.

未来方向：Expand the Cr-isotope survey to spatially-controlled ELIP feeder dykes and coeval picrites to map redox heterogeneity within the plume head; couple Cr isotopes with V-Li and S multi-isotope analyses to quantify the role of recycled sediments and pyroxenite in generating oxidised mantle domains.

研究相关性：For researchers modelling sulfide saturation in LIPs, the paper provides the first direct, isotope-based fO2 grid that can be plugged into thermodynamic simulations to predict metal endowment; the contrasting redox signatures of high-Ti vs low-Ti suites offer a new exploration vector when only drill-core or weathered surface samples are available.

Biogeochemical Controls on Volatile Sulfur Compounds in the Kuroshio‐Oyashio Confluence Region

黑潮-亲潮交汇区挥发性硫化物的生物地球化学控制

2026-01-02
Journal of Geophysical Research: Oceans (IF: 3.3)

Xuan‐Li Liu，Lei Li，Shan‐Shan Liu，Cheng‐Xuan Li，Gui‐Peng Yang

DOI: 10.1029/2025jc023091

Abstract Marine‐derived volatile sulfur compounds (VSCs) play a critical role in global climate regulation and the sulfur biogeochemical cycle. To characterize their biogeochemical dynamics, a comprehensive investigation on VSCs, including dimethyl sulfide (DMS), carbonyl sulfide (OCS), and carbon disulfide (CS 2 ), was conducted in the upper seawater and the overlying atmosphere in the Kuroshio‐Oyashio confluence region. The average concentrations of DMS, OCS and CS 2 were 1.03 ± 1.40, 0.075 ± 0.042, 0.029 ± 0.024 nmol L −1 in seawater, and 125.9 ± 58.9, 497.9 ± 157.1, 67.4 ± 56.9 pptv in the atmosphere, respectively. Mean sea‐to‐air fluxes of DMS, OCS and CS 2 were 5.67 ± 6.20, 0.151 ± 0.273, and 0.125 ± 0.148 μmol m −2 d −1 , respectively. The elevated OCS and CS 2 in subsurface waters of the Kuroshio Extension (KE) were attributed to phytoplankton abundance and temperature‐dependent hydrolysis. The production of DMS, as the chemical signaling molecule and antioxidant, was promoted in the KE and eddy‐active regions in warm but oligotrophic conditions. Co‐occurrence of high DMS and CS 2 concentrations with elevated chlorophyll levels in upper waters of the Oyashio Current highlighted the pivotal role of phytoplankton in the production of DMS and CS 2 . Notably, the edges of oceanic eddies were observed as hotspots for OCS and CS 2 . Mixing ratios of VSCs in the atmosphere were comprehensively modulated by sea‐air interactions, air mass transport, and oxidation processes. These findings provide valuable insights into the complex interplay between biological and physical processes that govern the biogeochemistry of VSCs in dynamic oceanic environments.
