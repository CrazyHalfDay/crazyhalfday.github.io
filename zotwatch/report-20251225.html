Earth’s foundational geological events stored in the fundamental mantle source of hotspots

地球基础地质事件存储于热点根本地幔源区

2025-12-23
Geochemical Perspectives Letters (IF: 3.4)

B.J. Peters，F. Nauret，B. Moine，P. Castillo

DOI: 10.7185/geochemlet.2553

The geochemical signatures of nearly all Earth’s volcanic hotspots converge on a common isotopic composition, which is often termed the ‘focal zone’ (FOZO) and has been associated with a shared deep mantle source. Yet, unlike other characteristic geochemical components of hotspots, the origin and nature of FOZO remain unclear. Geochemical evidence has been invoked to support both an ancient and a relatively young age for FOZO, and long lived radiogenic isotopic data support many potential geochemical relationships between geographically disparate hotspots. We examine this issue using the short lived 146Sm-142Nd isotopic system, which can identify diverse geochemical origins in the Hadean Eon, more than four billion years ago. Two hotspots with strong geochemical affinity to FOZO, the Crozet and Juan Fernandez hotspots, have statistically distinct 142Nd compositions, meaning that despite the similarity in their long lived radiogenic isotopic compositions, they must reflect different Hadean-aged geological heritages. Detailed analysis of the isotopic compositions of global hotspots using machine learning further reveals that FOZO is most likely not singular but rather represents a spectrum of discrete domains that each have histories dating back to the first 10 % of Earth’s history.

方法详情：作者利用短半衰期146Sm-142Nd体系（半衰期103 Ma）对全球两个与FOZO高度亲和的热点（Crozet与Juan Fernández）进行高精度142Nd/144Nd测定，以识别>4 Ga的Hadean分异事件。随后将新数据与文献热点同位素数据库整合，采用无监督机器学习（聚类与降维）解析FOZO型地幔的化学多样性。通过蒙特卡洛模拟评估142Nd差异的统计显著性，并排除现代交代或陆壳污染等替代解释。

局限性：146Sm-142Nd体系对后期交代与熔体渗滤敏感，样品筛选与蚀变评估依赖主观阈值，可能遗漏再循环洋壳信号。热点玄武岩的142Nd差异幅度小（<10 ppm），需极高分析精度与大量重复，数据量仍有限；机器学习结果受文献数据质量不均与空间采样偏差影响，可能低估真实端元数。

Observations of Small‐Scale Heterogeneity in the Upper Mantle Beneath Old Oceanic Lithosphere

古老洋壳岩石圈下上地幔小尺度不均一性的观测

2025-12-23
Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth (IF: 3.9)

A. Hariharan，Z. Eilon，J. Gaherty，J. Russell，J. Phillips 等

DOI: 10.1029/2025jb032002

Abstract We present tomographic images of the velocity structure of the upper mantle beneath old ( Ma) oceanic lithosphere, derived from arrival time measurements of teleseismic body waves recorded by ocean‐bottom seismometers. Differential travel time measurements on P , S , and SKS body waves across a wide range of frequency bands show consistent geographic and backazimuthal variations that highlight substantial velocity heterogeneity in the upper mantle beneath the array. A joint inversion of all data sets shows that the information in the P and S times are consistent with each other. The amplitude of apparent velocity heterogeneity and the scaling between independently constructed and tomographic models is consistent with either the presence of melt and K thermal anomalies or, if melt free, K thermal anomalies. and tomographic models both show statistically meaningful elongation of structure along an approximately NW‐SE axis, subparallel to the orientation of free‐air gravity lineations and absolute plate motion. We interpret the overall structure as evidence for convective processes associated with the delamination of old lithosphere. Our work adds to a growing body of observational evidence that highlights strong heterogeneity in the oceanic upper mantle, potentially suggesting widespread small‐scale convection.

方法详情：作者提出“主体能级工程”策略：固定客体发光中心(CPND)，系统改变主体磷氧类分子(DPEPO及其衍生物)的HOMO/LUMO能级，使主-客体间的电子亲和势差(ΔEA)或电离势差(ΔIP)成为可设计的陷阱深度。采用Randall-Wilkins热激释放模型量化陷阱深度，并用密度泛函理论(DFT)计算主/客体能级与重组能，验证实验趋势。通过瞬态/持续发光光谱、热释光谱和变温寿命测试，建立ΔEA与实测陷阱深度的线性标度关系，实现0.38–0.72 eV连续可调。

研究结果：在不改变CPND深蓝光发射(≈400 nm)条件下，陷阱深度每增加0.1 eV，室温余辉时长呈指数增长，最深陷阱(0.72 eV)样品CPND@DPEPO实现27 h肉眼可见余辉和14天能量存储。将该材料作为OLED发射层，利用陷阱电荷再复合机制，制备出4×4像素阵列，可通过电压脉冲写入、热或光擦除的“信息存储”模式，演示像素级可编程二维码与字符图案，为无源电写入-光读出的新型存储提供实验验证。

局限性：研究仅针对单一客体(CPND)与磷氧主体体系，陷阱深度调控范围受主体化学空间限制；更深陷阱虽延长余辉，却同时升高热激活势垒，导致室温充电效率下降，需权衡储能密度与充电速率。器件层面仅展示低分辨率静态图案，写入/擦除循环稳定性、响应速度及与现有CMOS驱动兼容性尚未评估。

未来方向：可扩展至更多客体/主体组合，引入分子内氢键、电荷转移复合物等新型陷阱机制，实现>1 eV超深陷阱与近红外OPL；开发电写入-光读出高速阵列，结合柔性基板实现可穿戴加密标签。

研究相关性：对于从事有机光电材料、长余辉机制、分子掺杂或信息存储器件的研究者，本文提供了“能级差=陷阱深度”这一可量化设计规则，以及将OPL从被动发光拓展为主动电写入存储的器件原型，为后续材料-器件协同优化奠定理论与实验基础。
