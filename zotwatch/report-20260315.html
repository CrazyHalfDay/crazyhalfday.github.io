Effect of Fe content on reduced partition function ratios of Ni isotopes in olivine: A first-principles study

Fe 含量对橄榄石中 Ni 同位素约化配分函数比的影响：一项第一性原理研究

2026-03-11
American Mineralogist (IF: 2.7)

Anwen Hou，Jiaxin Liang，Huiquan Tian，Shanqi Liu，Yongbing Li

DOI: 10.2138/am-2025-9946

Abstract Olivine is one of the most abundant minerals in the Earth’s mantle and is widely distributed in meteorites and mafic–ultramafic igneous rocks. In recent years, Ni isotopes in olivine have been extensively used to trace geological processes such as mantle evolution, crust-mantle interaction, ore formation, and planetary differentiation. However, the mechanism by which the Ni isotope fractionation properties of olivine respond to variations in Fe content remains poorly understood, limiting its effectiveness as a geochemical tracer. In this study, we employed first-principles calculations to determine the reduced partition function ratios of 60Ni/58Ni (β60-58 factors) in olivine with varying Fe contents. Our calculations show that, at a constant Ni concentration, increasing Fe content significantly expands the unit cell volume and lengthens the average Ni–O bond, which in turn leads to a decrease in the β60-58 factor. A significant negative correlation is observed between the average Ni–O bond length and the β60-58 factor. In contrast, when Ni/(Ni+Fe+Mg) ≤ 2/32, variations in Ni content have a negligible effect on the β60-58 factor. Given the generally low Ni concentrations in natural olivine, it is inferred that variations in δ60/58Ni values are unlikely to be influenced by Ni content. The results not only clarify the mechanism by which Fe content influences Ni isotope fractionation properties of olivine, but also provide essential data for the application of Ni isotopes in tracing geological evolution processes on Earth and in extraterrestrial bodies.

研究结果：研究表明，在Ni浓度保持恒定时，随着橄榄石中Fe含量升高，晶胞体积明显增大，平均Ni–O键长变长，而β60-58因子随之降低。这说明Fe富集会削弱Ni所处局部结构对重同位素的相对稳定作用，从而改变橄榄石的平衡Ni同位素分馏特征。作者进一步发现，平均Ni–O键长与β60-58因子之间存在显著负相关关系，表明Ni–O键长是控制该体系Ni同位素分馏的重要结构参数。相比之下，当Ni/(Ni+Fe+Mg) ≤ 2/32时，Ni含量变化对β60-58因子的影响很弱，这意味着天然橄榄石中普遍较低的Ni含量通常不会显著控制其δ60/58Ni变化，真正更关键的变量是Fe含量及其引起的局部结构改变。

局限性：首先，该研究主要基于理论计算与理想化晶体模型，虽然能够揭示机制，但与天然样品中复杂的成分不均一性、缺陷、微量元素耦合替代以及温压条件变化相比，仍存在简化。其次，论文摘要显示研究重点放在Fe和Ni含量变化对β因子的影响，尚未充分涉及温度、压力、氧逸度或不同晶体学占位偏好等因素对Ni同位素分馏的协同控制。最后，约化配分函数比反映的是平衡分馏潜力，而天然体系中常见的动力学过程、扩散效应和后期蚀变可能导致实际测得同位素组成偏离纯平衡预测。

未来方向：后续研究可将第一性原理结果与高温高压实验、天然样品原位分析相结合，检验Fe含量—Ni–O键长—β因子之间关系在真实地质条件下的适用性。进一步也可扩展到不同温压条件、不同橄榄石端元组成以及陨石和地幔岩体系中，以建立更完整的Ni同位素分馏数据库。

Diffusive copper redistribution from shallow degassed magmas to mafic enclaves. Insight from anomalously Cu-enriched enclaves from Bezymianny volcano, Kamchatka

铜从浅部脱气岩浆向镁铁质包体的扩散再分配——来自堪察加 Bezymianny 火山异常富铜包体的启示

2026-03-09
Journal of Volcanology and Geothermal Research (IF: 2.3)

Vesta O. Davydova，Vasily D. Shcherbakov，Pavel Yu. Plechov，Vasily O. Yapaskurt，Yuri D. Shcherbakov 等

DOI: 10.1016/j.jvolgeores.2026.108588

Mafic enclaves enriched in sulfides and copper compared to their host magmas are found in felsic plutons associated with porphyry deposits and in eruptive products of arc volcanoes. The origin of these Cu-rich enclaves is usually linked to either intrinsically Cu-rich parental magmas or to extensive fractionation of primitive magmas under sulfide-undersaturated conditions before they are stored in shallow felsic reservoirs. Using Bezymianny volcano as a case study, we suggest an alternative mechanism for the formation of Cu-rich mafic enclaves. Detailed petrographic analysis shows that sulfides in Bezymianny enclaves evolve from scarce early Cu-poor MSS (chilled monosulfide solid solution close to pyrrhotite or euhedral pyrrhotite with thin ISS rims) enclosed in phenocrysts, to Cu-rich ISS (intermediate solid solution close to cubanite) in matrix glass and vesicles. This increase in Cu content with crystallization requires the external addition of Cu. Mineral oxybarometry indicates that the shallow-stored magma (<1 kbar) had an oxygen fugacity of 0 to +1 log units above NNO. Intense degassing of S-bearing aqueous fluid kept sulfur concentrations in silicate melts below sulfide saturation, destabilizing sulfides and causing incompatible behavior of chalcophile elements. Consequently, Cu accumulated in silicate melt until inputs of sulfide-bearing mafic magma from deeper levels (9–2 kbar) triggered its diffusive redistribution into enclaves. Cu diffused from the Cu-rich, sulfide-free silicate melt of the shallow reservoir into the Cu-poor silicate melt of mafic enclaves, and subsequently into their sulfide phases. High diffusion rates of monovalent Cu allow small enclaves to achieve full Cu enrichment within several weeks.

Volatile resorption expedites eruption onset in large silicic systems

挥发分再吸收加速大型富硅岩浆系统喷发的启动

2026-03-12
Nature Communications (IF: 14.7)

Franziska Keller，Meredith Townsend，Juliana Troch，Christian Huber

DOI: 10.1038/s41467-026-70206-8

Abstract Silicic caldera-forming eruptions are among the most hazardous natural phenomena on Earth, yet their triggering mechanisms remain poorly understood. While volatile exsolution is widely recognized as a potential eruption driver in large silicic systems, we find that volatile resorption can, counterintuitively, promote chamber pressurization faster than volatile exsolution. Using a thermo-mechanical magma chamber model, we show that resorption is a common process in rapidly recharged systems, driven by pressure increase and crystal melting. The Aso-4 eruption offers a natural case where volatile resorption may have occurred, with model results predicting resorption at recharge rates >10 -2.4 km 3 /yr. Through reducing bulk magma compressibility, resorption amplifies pressurization, driving chamber destabilization and potentially expediting eruption onset. Here, we propose that volatile resorption is a natural process both accommodating and promoting rapid chamber pressurization, fundamental to destabilizing large-scale silicic systems. Detecting its signatures in monitoring signals could provide early warning of imminent eruption.
