研究背景：Continental mantle earthquakes are rare, yet they provide critical constraints on the rheology and stress state of the lithospheric upper mantle. The scarcity of well-located mantle events beneath stable continents leaves large gaps in our understanding of how deformation is accommodated at depth. The Canadian Rockies, an active orogen with ongoing convergence, offers a natural laboratory to detect and characterize such deep seismicity.

方法详情：The authors mined 25 years of continuous three-component seismic data from permanent and temporary networks across Alberta and British Columbia. They applied a matched-filter detection algorithm that cross-correlated template waveforms of previously catalogued upper-mantle earthquakes to discover 300 additional microearthquakes down to Mw 0.8. All events were relocated with a double-difference scheme using regional P- and S-arrivals and a 3-D velocity model derived from active-source and teleseismic tomography. Focal mechanisms were computed from first-motion polarities and full-waveform moment tensor inversion, and stress-drop estimates were obtained via spectral fitting of Brune-type source spectra.

研究结果：The relocated hypocenters define a 30-km-thick, sub-horizontal shear zone centered at ~75 km depth within the uppermost mantle, directly beneath the crustal root of the Canadian Rockies. Focal mechanisms show consistent E-W to ESE-WNW thrusting with P-axes aligned parallel to present-day Pacific-North America convergence, indicating that the mantle lithosphere is actively shortening. Stress drops (1–20 MPa) and b-values (0.7) are indistinguishable from those of crustal intraplate earthquakes, implying similar brittle failure despite higher temperature and pressure. The discovery demonstrates that seismic strain in orogens can localize in the mantle and that the continental lithosphere can support significant differential stress to depths of at least 90 km.

局限性：Detection is limited to events larger than Mw ~0.8, so the complete magnitude–frequency distribution and total moment budget remain uncertain. The 3-D velocity model lacks resolution directly within the shear zone, introducing location uncertainties of ±3–5 km that hinder precise determination of shear-zone thickness and dip. No geodynamic modeling is performed to test whether the seismicity reflects localized dehydration, grain-size reduction, or thermal shear instability.

未来方向：Dense nodal arrays should be deployed to lower the magnitude detection threshold to Mw −1 and map microseismicity at meter-scale precision. Coupled thermo-mechanical modeling that incorporates the new rheological constraints could quantify the stress and temperature conditions required to sustain brittle failure in the uppermost mantle.

研究相关性：Researchers investigating lithospheric rheology, deep continental seismicity, or the mechanical behavior of mantle lithosphere in convergent settings will find this study a key reference for how modern detection techniques can reveal hidden deformation zones and for quantitative estimates of mantle stress levels.

研究背景：Granitic plutons are traditionally viewed as having crystallized entirely from silicate melt, but growing evidence suggests that late-stage, magmatic–hydrothermal transition zones can control ore-metal solubility and pegmatite formation. Quantifying the P–T–X conditions at which melt gives way to aqueous fluid is therefore critical for understanding granite-related mineralization, yet few studies have combined thermobarometry with trace-element and fluid-inclusion constraints in shallow-level intrusions.

方法详情：The authors applied multiple independent thermobarometers (Ti-in-quartz, Zr-in-rutile, and Al-in-hornblende) to early- and late-crystallizing mineral assemblages in the Spirit Mountain granite, Nevada, and complemented these with quartz-hosted fluid-inclusion microthermometry and trace-element laser-ablation profiles. Crystallization temperatures were further refined using feldspar–melt thermometry and Fe–Ti oxide geothermometry, while pressures were cross-checked against contact-metamorphic mineral equilibria in the aureole. Finally, textural criteria (graphic quartz–feldspar intergrowths, miarolitic cavities) were used to discriminate magmatic versus hydrothermal crystallization domains.

研究结果：Results record a continuous temperature decline from ~750 °C at 1.2 kbar (solidus front) to 520–480 °C at 0.5–0.3 kbar, coinciding with a sharp increase in fluid inclusion salinity (from 5–8 to 35–45 wt % NaCl eq.) and trace-element signatures indicative of aqueous fluid segregation. The transition occurs while 15–20 % residual melt remains, implying that brine exsolution begins before complete solidification and triggers rapid quartz + feldspar regrowth in miarolitic cavities. This finding links shallow emplacement, rapid decompression, and fluid saturation to the generation of high-salinity magmatic–hydrothermal ore fluids.

局限性：The study relies on a single pluton; therefore, the reported P–T path may not be universally applicable to other shallow granites with different water contents or ascent rates. Re-equilibration of fluid inclusions at low temperature and potential post-entrapment H loss from quartz could shift calculated salinities and pressures. Additionally, the thermobarometers assume equilibrium and may not capture transient fluctuations during episodic fluid expulsion.

未来方向：Comparable multi-method investigations should be extended to compositionally diverse, mineralized and barren shallow plutons to test whether the documented transition is a first-order control on metal endowment. Coupling high-resolution trace-element mapping with in-situ U–Pb dating of hydrothermal minerals could quantify the absolute timing and duration of the magmatic–hydrothermal switch.

研究相关性：Researchers studying granite-related Sn–W–Mo porphyry, rare-element pegmatite, or epithermal Au–Ag systems can use the integrated thermobarometric–fluid inclusion approach to pinpoint when and where metal-bearing brines exsolve, refining exploration vectors within shallow granitic terranes.

Fingerprinting fluctuations in auriferous fluid chemistry in pyrite zonation: Implications for ore-forming processes

黄铁矿环带中金成矿流体化学变化的指纹追踪：对成矿过程的启示

2026-01-06
Geological Society of America Bulletin (IF: 3.9)

Jia-Nan Fu，Hao-Cheng Yu，Zi-Yue Gao，Christof Kusebauch，Xin Liu 等

DOI: 10.1130/b38578.1

Gold can be hosted by pyrite in a multiplicity of hydrothermal ore systems, but the processes controlling the precipitation of Au and its incorporation into pyrite remain unclear. Here, we present a detailed petrographic, geochemical, and theoretical study of pyrite from the Liba gold deposit, China, using both trace element chemistry and sulfur isotopes. Gold-bearing pyrite grains from Liba record a compositional zonation including a Co-Ni−rich, an As-Au−poor core (Py-1), an As-rich mantle (Py-2), and finally an outmost rim (Py-3) enriched only in Co and Ni. An As-Au-Cu−rich thin zone (Py-2a) with numerous fine pores and arsenopyrite inclusions exists between Py-2 and Py-3. The range of sulfur isotopes is narrow, showing a gradually decreasing trend from Py-1 (mean = +9.57‰) to Py-1a (mean = +8.45‰), Py-2 (mean = +7.82‰), and Py-2a (mean = +6.82‰) and an increase in Py-3 (mean = +9.38‰), which is similar to Py-1. We interpret the periodic cycling of trace elements and sulfur isotopes as indicative of multiple, consecutive fluid-release events that tapped one single reservoir, which led to the formation of zoned pyrite. The elevated As and depleted S contents in Py-2 potentially indicate a change in sulfur fugacity, which would have led to a decreased solubility of Au in the hydrothermal fluid. Elemental variations from Py-2 to Py-2a (i.e., Au/Cu ratios ranging from 0.01 to 1) and the presence of pyrrhotite in Py-2a suggest that mineralization occurred under reducing conditions. Lower fO2 and fS2 would lead to gold supersaturation in the fluid, triggering Au precipitation in Py-2a. Additionally, coupled dissolution and reprecipitation reactions are found to play a role during the formation of key features observed in Liba pyrites (e.g., formation of porosity and inclusions), which promote fluid evolution. In addition, theoretical evaluation reveals that Cu incorporation may create conditions favorable for Au uptake into the pyrite structure, triggering As-Au-Cu enrichment in the rims. Our use of multiple scales of observation provides significant insights into the complex formation processes of mineralized hydrothermal systems.

Pb isotope and sequential extraction constraints on metal sources of Mississippi Valley−type Zn-Pb deposits in the Lanping fold-thrust belt, Eastern Himalayan-Tibetan orogen (Southwest China)

2026-01-06
Geological Society of America Bulletin (IF: 3.9)

Longlong Yue，Yingchao Liu，Jianxiong Ma，Aihua Wang，Yueyue Kang 等

DOI: 10.1130/b38395.1

Metals for Mississippi Valley−type Zn-Pb mineralization were previously considered to be leached from regional shallow sedimentary strata and basement rocks. However, determining the specific metal source is challenging because of the complex fluid interaction histories in Mississippi Valley−type ore fields. To better constrain their metal sources, we conducted a comprehensive study of the Lanping ore field of Southwest China. This ore field hosts the world-class Jinding Mississippi Valley−type deposit and Baiyangping Mississippi Valley−type ore district. The latter comprises numerous smaller deposits. All these deposits formed during the Cenozoic Era and are situated within the Cenozoic fold-thrust belt of the Eastern Himalayan-Tibetan orogenic belt. Sequential extraction of regional sedimentary strata (Permian to Eocene) targeted four fractions (carbonate, Fe-Mn oxide, organic and reductive, and residual silicate). This study, combined with leaching experiments on Triassic magmatic basement rocks and Cenozoic high-K plutons, demonstrates that magmatic rocks constitute the key metal sources, particularly through feldspar dissolution. Pb isotope data indicate that most fractions extracted from sedimentary rocks deviate from the linear Pb isotope trend observed in the Jinding sulfides. The lower intercept of this trend aligns with lower Cretaceous Jingxing Formation muddy siltstone, while the upper intercept extends to middle Triassic dacite K-feldspar. Model ages and linear mixing models indicate that Triassic felsic−mafic magmatic rocks were significant radiogenic Pb-bearing metal sources for Jinding, while lower Cretaceous siltstone or an end member 213 Ma or younger provided common Pb-bearing metals. The Pb isotopes of Baiyangping sulfides partially overlap with the gabbro, biotite monzogranite, and granite porphyry of Cenozoic high-K plutons and the Eocene Baoxiangsi Formation’s argillaceous fine sandstone fractions. These results indicate that the voluminous Triassic felsic−mafic rocks, which served as the basement for the entire Mesozoic Lanping Basin and rolled into the Cenozoic fold-thrust belt, have undergone large-scale fluid-rock reactions with the regional basin brines, providing a plentiful supply of Zn-Pb metals for regional Mississippi Valley−type deposits. In contrast, Cenozoic high-K plutons may intrude into more restricted spatial domains and might have undergone smaller-scale fluid-rock interactions, providing less Zn-Pb metals. This explains why a world-class Zn-Pb deposit formed at Jinding, while only small-scale Zn-Pb deposits developed at the Baiyangping ore district. This study illustrates that metal sources for Mississippi Valley−type Zn-Pb mineralization commonly display composite provenance characteristics, integrating contributions from sedimentary successions and basement rocks. In addition, it underscores the importance of metal release through interactions between fluids and magmatic rocks for the formation of large-scale Zn-Pb mineralization and demonstrates the utility of Pb isotope model ages in evaluating metal sources.

This study illustrates that metal sources for Mississippi Valley−type Zn-Pb mineralization commonly display composite provenance characteristics, integrating contributions from sedimentary successions and basement rocks. Apatite is a key accessory phase in metamorphic rocks, capable of recording mineral growth, deformation, and fluid-rock interaction across a wide range of metamorphic conditions. In high-pressure granulite-facies samples, apatite grains display strong shape-preferred orientation (SPO) and significant intragranular deformation but lack a crystallographic preferred orientation (CPO), consistent with pre-peak metamorphism growth and experienced peak metamorphic conditions. Medium-pressure granulite-facies apatite shows oscillatory zoning, weak SPO and CPO, and limited deformation, indicating post-tectonic growth from compositionally homogeneous fluids. Apatite from upper amphibolite-facies samples preserves both strong SPO and CPO, with only minor deformation features, suggesting syn-tectonic crystallization during foliation development. In lower amphibolite-facies rocks, apatite is characterized by core-mantle structures, weak SPO, but CPO subparallel to stretching lineation, and variable trace element patterns, consistent with episodic growth during fluid-mediated dissolution–precipitation under retrograde conditions. Systematic variations in apatite chemistry—including decreasing Sr/Y, ΣLREE, and (La/Yb)N, and increasing Th/U and F/Cl with decreasing metamorphic grade—reflect the influence of metamorphic fluid composition, mineral reaction pathways, and elemental partition among mineral phases at corresponding metamorphic conditions. Despite differences in deformation and growth history, consistent correlations between texture and composition suggest localized, short-range fluid-mediated element redistribution. Our results demonstrate that multiple generations of apatite can be distinguished through integrated microstructural and geochemical datasets, providing a robust archive of pressure–temperature (P–T) conditions and fluid evolution in metamorphic terranes.

研究结果：所得纤维拉伸强度达958 MPa，为迄今MXene纤维最高纪录，同时保持13 692 S cm⁻¹的高电导率；由该纤维编织的大面积织物在厚度仅约0.1 mm时电磁屏蔽效能达6 509 dB cm⁻¹，显著优于现有碳/金属基纺织屏蔽材料。

局限性：研究仅使用Ti₃C₂Tₓ一种MXene，未验证方法对其他组分或表面化学的普适性；湿纺-拉伸工艺依赖Ca²⁺-海藻酸瞬态凝胶网络，纤维在强酸/螯合环境中可能失去致密结构；超高拉伸比可能限制纤维连续长度与生产速度。

未来方向：可探索无离子或可交联聚合物替代海藻酸，提升环境稳定性；将径向约束-轴向拉伸策略扩展至Mo₂CTₓ、Ti₂CTₓ等MXene及其他二维材料体系，实现多组分复合纤维的连续化制备。

研究相关性：该工作提供了室温下二维片层致密化与取向的普适策略，为开发高强高导柔性纤维、智能织物及电磁屏蔽材料的研究者提供了可直接借鉴的工艺参数与性能基准。
