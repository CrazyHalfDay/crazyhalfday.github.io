Carbonated mantle peridotites represent a hidden sink for subducted CO2

碳酸化的地幔橄榄岩是俯冲CO2的隐藏汇/储库

2026-02-27
Nature Communications (IF: 14.7)

Elliot J. Carter，Brian O’Driscoll，Ray Burgess，Patricia L. Clay，Hélène Balcone-Boissard 等

DOI: 10.1038/s41467-026-68646-3

Abstract Subduction of carbon rich sediments and crust at convergent plate boundaries exerts a crucial control on Earth’s mantle chemistry and surface habitability. Recent attention has focused on exposures of fully-carbonated mantle rocks as these may attest to an overlooked sink for subducted carbon not sampled by arc volcanism. However, even in the best-studied example, the Semail Ophiolite, Oman, the setting for carbonation remains highly contentious, with conflicting inferences from geochemistry and geochronology. We approach this problem by combining microanalysis of halogens and detailed petrography to fingerprint the origins of carbonating fluids. Fluids were derived from both sedimentary pore fluid expulsion and deep slab decarbonation reactions in a subduction zone setting. Through mass balance modelling we show that CO 2 fluxes into the forearc from deep decarbonation (1.7–3.4 × 10 13 gyr −1 C) could represent up to 90% of the global flux entering subduction zones, indicating that carbonated mantle peridotites likely represent a major sink for subducted CO 2 which may have varied through geological time.

研究问题：碳酸化地幔橄榄岩是否为俯冲CO2隐藏汇及其流体来源？

研究方法：卤素微区分析结合岩相学，并用质量平衡计算CO2通量。

主要发现：孔隙水与深部脱碳供流体，前弧固碳通量或占全球俯冲输入90%。

创新点：以卤素地球化学指纹判别碳化流体并约束前弧固碳规模。

相关性：为俯冲带碳预算、地幔化学演化与地球宜居性提供新约束。

研究背景：俯冲带将富碳沉积物与洋壳带入地幔，是控制地幔碳循环、挥发分组成及地表宜居性的关键过程。已有研究提出“完全碳酸盐化”的地幔橄榄岩可能是俯冲 CO2 的重要隐蔽汇，但在典型区域（如阿曼 Semail 蛇绿岩）中，碳酸盐化发生的构造环境与流体来源长期存在争议且地球化学与年代学结论不一致。该研究的动机是用新的示踪手段对碳酸盐化流体进行“指纹识别”，从而约束 CO2 在前弧/地幔楔中的去向与规模。

方法详情：研究将卤素（主要为 Cl、Br、I 等）微区分析与精细岩相学/矿物学观察相结合，利用卤素在不同流体库（沉积物孔隙流体、海水改造流体、深部板片脱碳流体等）中的特征比值与含量差异来追踪流体来源与演化。通过显微结构与矿物共生关系重建碳酸盐化反应路径（如蛇纹石化与碳酸盐化的先后关系、裂隙充填与交代边界等），以辨别流体进入的时间—空间模式。随后使用质量守恒/通量模型，将推断的流体端元与岩石碳含量变化联系起来，估算来自深部脱碳的 CO2 进入前弧并被橄榄岩固定的通量范围。整体策略是用“微观示踪 + 岩相约束 + 通量核算”将现场观察与全球碳循环量级相连接。

研究结果：卤素微量组成与岩相证据共同表明，导致地幔橄榄岩碳酸盐化的流体并非单一来源，而是同时包含沉积物孔隙流体挤出（相对浅部、与沉积物相关）以及深部板片脱碳反应产生的流体/流体相（更深部、与变质脱碳相关）的贡献。由此推断碳酸盐化更符合俯冲带前弧—地幔楔环境下的多阶段流体渗入与交代，而非仅靠海底/大洋中脊或晚期地表过程即可解释。质量平衡建模给出：来自深部脱碳进入前弧的碳通量约为 1.7–3.4 × 10^13 g/yr C，可能高达全球进入俯冲带碳通量的 ~90%。这一结果意味着“碳酸盐化地幔橄榄岩”可能是俯冲 CO2 的主要隐蔽汇，从而解释为何弧火山逸散并未反映全部俯冲碳输入，并提示该隐蔽汇的强度可能随地质时期（温度结构、俯冲物质组成、流体通道化程度）显著变化。

局限性：研究的关键推断依赖于卤素作为流体示踪剂的端元假设与保存性：后期改造（变质再平衡、流体再循环、开放体系扩散或交代）可能部分重置卤素信号并增加端元混合的不确定性。通量估算建立在局部岩体（以 Semail 蛇绿岩/类似暴露为代表）的代表性、碳固定效率、碳酸盐化体积与反应程度等参数上，向全球外推可能存在区域偏倚。另一个潜在问题是时间尺度约束：若碳酸盐化是多期事件，不同期次的流体来源和通量可能难以在同一套样品中完全分离，从而影响对“深部脱碳贡献比例”的定量精度。

未来方向：可在不同俯冲带前弧/蛇绿岩体系中开展同位素（C-O-Sr、B、Cl）与卤素联合示踪，并与高精度年代学耦合，以检验该机制的普适性与随俯冲热结构变化的响应。进一步将反应-运移数值模拟与野外体积约束结合，可更稳健地评估碳酸盐化地幔作为全球碳汇的时空分布与动态通量。

研究问题：科马提岩与橄榄岩反应能否致克拉通岩石圈富硅？

研究方法：高温高压反应熔融实验结合矿物/熔体成分分析。

主要发现：科马提岩熔体渗入可生成富Si熔体并使橄榄岩富正辉石。

创新点：首次用实验定量约束该反应的Si迁移与相变路径。

相关性：为解释克拉通地幔富硅及其演化、地球化学信号提供机制。

研究背景：太古宙克拉通岩石圈地幔常表现出相对“富硅/富斜方辉石”的特征（如高 Opx/Ol 比），但其成因可来自初始熔融残余、后期交代改造或熔体-岩石反应等多种过程。由于科马提岩（komatiite）代表极高温、低黏度、强反应性的幔源熔体，作者关注“科马提质熔体与橄榄岩反应”是否能够有效在克拉通岩石圈中引入 SiO2 并改变矿物组合，从而解释富硅特征与稳定性问题。

Growing concerns about plastic pollution and climate change worldwide are driving research into the effects of microplastics on carbon cycling. Although the impacts of microplastics on soil organic carbon (SOC) levels and carbon emissions have been demonstrated, their role in SOC sequestration remains understudied. Here we discuss how interactions between microplastics and organic matter may affect SOC sequestration through biological, geochemical and physical processes. Microplastics and co-occurring contaminants alter SOC content and quality by leaching dissolved organic matter (DOM) and affecting native SOC mineralization. These changes may influence the efficacy of the microbial carbon pump and mineral-associated organic carbon formation. Furthermore, microplastic surfaces adsorb DOM for long-term preservation and form hotspots for mineral–organic matter interactions, thereby altering carbon storage within soil aggregates. Consequently, a comprehensive understanding of the role of microplastics in soil carbon dynamics, from local to global scales, is essential to address both microplastic pollution and carbon neutrality. Most global niobium (Nb) resources are associated with carbonatite systems and are commonly interpreted as magmatic in origin, with Nb hosted mainly by pyrochlore [(Na,Ca) 2 Nb 2 O 6 F] crystallized from carbonatitic melts. However, in parts of the giant Bayan Obo REE–Nb deposit, Nb occurs predominantly as columbite [FeNb 2 O 6 ] within fenite rather than carbonatite, implying an underappreciated hydrothermal style of Nb mineralization. This study integrates mineralogical observations, geochemical analyses, and hydrothermal experiments to investigate the origin of the fenite-hosted Nb ores and to advance our understanding of Nb enrichment behavior in carbonatite-related systems. The Nb-rich fenite (up to 8216 ppm Nb) mainly contains biotite, accompanied by subordinate columbite, calcite, barite, monazite, bastnäsite, and pyrite. It is enriched in fluid-mobile elements (e.g., Rb up to 297 ppm, Ba up to 17,552 ppm, and S up to 13.35 wt% as SO 3 ), indicating its formation through intensive interaction between carbonatite-derived fluids and silicate wall rocks. Biotite Ti thermometry suggests a high temperature (> 600 °C) of fenitizing fluids. The mineral assemblages indicate that these fluids were enriched in K + and CO 3 2– , with subordinate F − . Based on these physicochemical constraints, hydrothermal diamond anvil cell (HDAC) experiments were conducted, and demonstrate that columbite can be effectively dissolved in K + –CO 3 2– –F - -bearing fluids at temperatures of ∼ 400–∼600 °C. In general, this study shows that carbonatite-related Nb mineralization can extend beyond the purely magmatic stage into a high-temperature hydrothermal stage, highlighting the exploration potential of fenite-hosted hydrothermal Nb resources.
