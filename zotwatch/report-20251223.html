Geologically constrained data-driven modeling for mineral prospectivity mapping

地质约束的数据驱动建模在矿产资源远景预测中的应用

2025-12-22
Geology (IF: 4.8)

Luoqi Wang，Tianyi Li，Sensen Wu，Jie Yang，Yanhua Hu 等

DOI: 10.1130/g53947.1

The discovery and development of mineral resources are critical for sustaining modern energy demands. However, the geological processes that control mineralization are inherently complex, introducing considerable spatial variability that presents significant challenges for predictive modeling. While machine learning approaches have been increasingly applied to mineral prospectivity, many fail to explicitly incorporate key geological constraints, limiting their capacity to resolve the nonlinear and directionally dependent nature of mineralizing systems. Here we present a geologically constrained data-driven method that explicitly accounts for the spatial non-stationarity and anisotropy in ore-forming processes. In the benchmark case study from Canada, our method demonstrates a 7.4% improvement in recall performance compared with existing models. This robust performance is also observed in applications to the southern Cordillera region. Furthermore, the method elucidates regional ore-forming controls and quantifies spatial anisotropy in porphyry copper systems. Our findings demonstrate that incorporating geological constraints into data-driven models enhances both the accuracy and interpretability of mineral prospectivity assessments, offering a robust path forward in resource exploration.

Silver isotope fractionation by chemical diffusion in granitic melt

花岗质熔体中化学扩散导致的银同位素分馏

2025-12-22
Geochemical Perspectives Letters (IF: 3.4)

S. Li，L. Zhang，Y. Fang，H. Ni，F. Huang

DOI: 10.7185/geochemlet.2552

We report the first study of silver (Ag) isotope fractionation during diffusion in anhydrous granitic melts using diffusion couple experiments. The mass dependence of the diffusion coefficients of the Ag isotopes can be described by D109Ag/D107Ag = (107/109)β. In this study, we performed piston-cylinder experiments to constrain the β-factor for Ag isotopic fractionation by diffusion in anhydrous granitic melts. By regression and error estimation of the diffusive isotope profiles, we obtained β-values of 0.317 ± 0.036 and 0.274 ± 0.016 for the two diffusion experiments. These β-values are the largest in metal stable isotopes reported for silicate melts, reflecting rapid diffusion and minimal interaction of Ag+ with the melt network. A theoretical model was established based on experimental data, predicting significant diffusive Ag isotope fractionation (>6 ‰) during granitic magma ascent and fluid exsolution. The modelling results suggest that Ag isotopes can be used to trace silver migration, magma mixing and mineralisation processes.

局限性：实验仅限无水花岗质成分，未考察水含量、氧逸度及Cl、S等配位体影响；扩散时间较短，可能未完全达到稳态同位素分馏。此外，β值外推到天然岩浆–热液系统需验证压力与温度依赖性。

研究相关性：对于研究金属稳定同位素分馏机制、岩浆–热液成矿过程或利用同位素示踪金属来源与迁移路径的学者，该文提供了迄今最精确的Ag扩散β值与可直接套用的分馏模型。

Sodic nature of carbonatite melts and the origin of calcite versus dolomite carbonatites

碳酸岩熔体的钠质特征及方解石与白云石碳酸岩的成因

2025-12-22
Geology (IF: 4.8)

Simone Marioni，Gino Sartori，Max W. Schmidt

DOI: 10.1130/g53916.1

Carbonatites are magmatic rocks that host most economic rare earth element (REE) deposits and bear on the deep Earth volatile cycle. Yet, the cumulative character of plutonic carbonatites and the rapid posteruptive alteration of volcanic carbonatites impede a direct determination of the melt compositions crystallizing these rocks. This study focused on sodium in carbonatite melts, a critical component for their genesis, magmatic evolution, and also for REE mobility, given that most carbonatite-hosted REE mineralizations are formed by alkali-rich fluids. Through experiments at subvolcanic conditions (100−200 MPa, 800−1000 °C), we determined the partition coefficient of Na between apatite and carbonatite melt, DNaapatite/carbonatite, which is rather uniformly 0.010 ± 0.003 at temperatures ≥800 °C. 研究背景：地震活动是否受固体潮调制是地球物理学悬而未决的经典问题，其答案可揭示控制断层失稳的深部物理性质与触发机制。南加州拥有全球密度最高的地震台网与四十年连续记录，为在区域尺度重新审视该问题提供了独特窗口。

方法详情：作者整合南加州地震台网1981–2021年目录（ completeness magnitude ∼ M 2.0 ）与2010–2020年 Quake Template Matching 高分辨率模板匹配目录（ completeness magnitude ∼ M 1.2 ），共约 1.1 × 10 6 个事件。对每 0.1 ∘ × 0.1 ∘ 空间网格，计算固体潮正应力、剪应力及库仑应力随时间序列，并采用 Schuster 检验与叠加-倾角法量化地震发生率与相位相关性；随后反演得到各网格的有效背景正应力 σ e f f 作为拟合参数。

研究结果：研究发现潮汐调制强度呈显著空间差异，仅 7 % 的区域通过 95 % 置信检验，这些区域对应 σ e f f = 20 – 155 kPa 的地热区，远低于周围背景值。在部分断层段，≥ 8 km 的深部地震比浅部事件表现出更高潮汐相关性（ p < 0.01 ）。走滑与逆冲型地震在特定构造环境下分别对潮汐剪应力与正应力更敏感，表明断层几何与应力轴夹角决定潮汐加载效率。结果支持“低有效应力断层更易受微小应力扰动触发”的理论，并提供用地震目录直接估算深部 σ e f f 的新探针。

局限性：研究仍受限于目录完整性随时间变化，早期 M < 2.5 事件漏检可能低估相关性；未考虑孔隙流体压力潮汐分量及浅层非构造诱发地震；网格尺度 0.1 ∘ 仍可能平均掉断层尺度的应力异质性。

未来方向：未来可结合分布式声学传感（DAS）与机器学习再定位，将 completeness 降至 M 0 并扩展至 3-D 应力张量，检验本研究提出的 σ e f f 估算在其它俯冲带与火山区的普适性。
