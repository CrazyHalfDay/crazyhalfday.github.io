Sublithospheric diamond constraints on the state of deeply subducted slabs

岩石圈下方钻石对深部俯冲板片状态的约束

2026-05-15
Science Advances (IF: 11.7)

Qiwei Zhang，Frank E. Brenker，Suzette Timmerman，Steven B. Shirey，Thomas Stachel 等

DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.adz7447

Deep slab subduction is vital to Earth’s geochemical cycles, but relevant geodynamic models lack natural mineralogical constraints. We report lower mantle inclusion assemblages in diamond carrying the geochemical signature of subducted slab mantle. Retrogressed stishovite implies its entrapment at temperatures at least 500°C cooler than ambient lower mantle. Three coexisting tuite inclusions with radiogenic strontium isotopic compositions and enriched trace element patterns suggest phosphorus-rich, slab crust–derived carbonatitic melts metasomatized the mantle of a buckled and overturned slab. Survival of carbonatitic crustal sources to lower mantle depths supports the cold slab temperatures implied by the stishovite. Cold slab temperatures produce a bimineralic stishovite-ferropericlase slab, a wholly new mantle lithology with elevated density and seismic velocity that is distinctly higher than slabs dominated by olivine high-pressure polymorphs. This new deep slab mineralogy likely controls the penetration of cold slabs into the lower mantle.

方法详情：研究以携带深部包裹体的金刚石为核心样品，通过识别其中的下地幔矿物组合来重建包裹体被捕获时的成因环境。作者重点分析了退变的斯石英（stishovite）和三颗共生的 tuite 包裹体，并结合矿物学特征、显微结构、微量元素分布及锶同位素组成，判断这些包裹体是否具有俯冲板片来源的地球化学指纹。通过将包裹体矿物组合与高压相稳定关系、下地幔环境温度以及板片组成模型进行对比，作者进一步推断包裹体捕获时的温度显著低于周围地幔。最后，研究将矿物学与地球动力学解释联系起来，提出一种由斯石英和铁方镁石主导的冷俯冲板片新型岩性，并讨论其密度和地震波速度效应。

未来方向：未来可通过扩大超深金刚石包裹体样本库、结合更高分辨率的同位素与微量元素分析，检验这种冷板片矿物组合在不同俯冲体系中的普遍性。进一步将实验岩石学、第一性原理矿物物性计算与全球地震层析成像联合起来，也有助于定量评估这种新型板片岩性对下地幔结构与板片停滞/穿透行为的控制。

Anisotropic Permeability Pathways Controlled by Intraslab Stress Heterogeneity in the Philippine Sea Slab Beneath the Ryukyu Arc

琉球弧下方菲律宾海板块内板内应力非均一性控制的各向异性渗透路径

2026-05-16
Geochemistry, Geophysics, Geosystems (IF: 3.1)

Makoto Otsubo，Ayumu Miyakawa

DOI: 10.1029/2025gc012607

Abstract Intermediate‐depth intraslab earthquakes in the Philippine Sea Plate occur at depths of 100–200 km beneath the Ryukyu Arc and are widely interpreted as manifestations of slab dehydration; however, the spatial variability in continuity of the volcanic front and the asymmetric distribution of volcanic activity are poorly explained by existing models. We demonstrate that spatial variations in the intraslab stress field control anisotropy in the permeability of the subducted slab, influencing the up‐dip migration of fluids and therefore the distribution of arc volcanism. By inverting the stress tensors of 184 focal mechanism solutions ( M w > 3.3) of earthquakes between 1997 and 2007, we identified heterogeneous intraslab stress regimes and evaluated how their mechanical expression, particularly the orientation of the intermediate principal stress axis ( σ 2 ), influences the direction of fluid flow. Our results show that regions characterized by down‐dip extensional stress regimes, in which σ 2 is oriented approximately perpendicular to the slab dip, coincide with enhanced permeability and efficient dehydration pathways toward the mantle wedge. These results highlight the pivotal role of intraslab stress in shaping fluid migration pathways and arc magmatism in the Ryukyu subduction zone.

主要发现：下倾向伸展且σ2近垂直板片倾向的区域具有更强脱水通道与火山活动。

相关性：为解释俯冲带流体迁移、岩浆作用及火山前缘不连续性提供新机制。

研究背景：该研究聚焦于琉球弧下方菲律宾海板块内100–200 km深度的中深源板内地震，这类地震通常被解释为俯冲板块脱水作用的表现。然而，现有脱水—火山作用模型难以解释琉球弧火山前缘连续性差以及火山活动空间分布明显不对称的现象。作者因此提出，有必要从板块内部应力非均一性出发，重新审视流体迁移通道及其对弧火山作用的控制机制。

方法详情：研究基于1997至2007年间菲律宾海板块内184个震级Mw > 3.3的地震震源机制解，对琉球俯冲带中深源板内地震的应力张量进行了反演，以识别不同区域的板内应力状态。作者重点分析了主应力轴的空间变化，尤其是中间主应力轴σ2的方位，因为其被认为与裂隙开启方向及渗透率各向异性密切相关。在此基础上，研究将应力场类型与板块脱水产生流体的上倾迁移路径联系起来，评估不同应力体制下板块内流体能否更有效地向地幔楔输运。方法上本质上是将地震学约束的应力反演结果与岩石力学中“应力控制裂隙连通性和渗透率”的概念结合，用来解释火山活动的空间分布差异。

Abstract The plate boundary in a subduction zone is a narrow, weak shear zone, which transitions from brittle to ductile deformation with increasing depth.
