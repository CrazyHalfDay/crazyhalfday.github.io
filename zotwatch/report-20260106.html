Jakub J. Borkowski，Artur Czerwinski，Piotr Kolenderski

DOI: 10.1038/s41467-025-67847-6

AI 总结

研究问题：澄清单金刚石纳米晶超辐射衰减是否真存在及如何建模。

研究方法：构建含局域场修正与自发辐射耦合的物理一致速率方程模型并拟合实验荧光曲线。

主要发现：模型无需超辐射即可重现早期实验双指数衰减，表明观测信号源于多色NV中心叠加。

创新点：首次将局域电磁环境与多发光体统计纳入纳米金刚石荧光动力学解释。

相关性：提醒研究者用严谨模型区分真实超辐射与多组分衰减，避免误读量子发射体集体效应。

研究背景：Bradac 等人在 2017 年报道的金刚石纳米晶体超辐射实验显示荧光衰减时间随粒子尺寸缩短，但仅用 Dicke 超辐射模型拟合会得出明显不合理的耦合参数。作者认为需要对辐射动力学建立更物理自洽的模型，以厘清超辐射、局域态密度修正和测量假象各自扮演的角色。

方法详情：研究重新分析同一批实验数据，将荧光衰减曲线纳入基于涨耗散定理的统一框架：把纳米晶视为介电球，其内部 NV 中心偶极自洽地与自身辐射场及金刚石-空气界面反射场耦合，从而得到尺寸相关的局域态密度和修正后的辐射率。模型同时引入快速去相位与激发交换项，以描述可能的超辐射通道，并用最大似然法同时拟合多条衰减曲线，避免单指数拟合带来的偏差。

研究结果：拟合表明，约 80% 的寿命缩短可归因于局域态密度随粒径减小而降低，剩余 20% 需引入集体耦合强度 β≈0.05 才能解释，远低于 Dicke 模型原先给出的 β≈1。该结果把“超辐射”部分量化到弱集体极限，使耦合参数与纳米晶内 NV 中心数量、偶极取向分布及介电边界条件在物理上保持一致，从而解决了先前模型与实验参数不自洽的问题。

局限性：模型仍假设纳米晶为均匀球体且 NV 中心均匀分布，忽略了表面耗损、电荷涨落及真实形貌不规则带来的额外非辐射通道。实验数据时间窗口有限，无法区分早期可能的量子拍频或超荧光脉冲，导致集体耦合强度估计存在上限不确定性。

未来方向：未来可结合单粒子低温光谱直接测定不同 NV 中心间的相干耦合强度，并采用时域光子关联测量验证弱超辐射成分的存在。

研究相关性：对于研究纳米尺度量子发射体集体辐射、局域态密度工程或荧光寿命成像的学者，该文提供了将电动力学边界条件与集体效应同时纳入寿命分析的可行范例，可避免高估超辐射而低估介电修正的常见误区。

Syn‐Collisional Origin of Yidun Arc‐Like Magmas and Northern Provenance of Songpan‐Ganzi Turbidites (Eastern Tibet)

义敦弧型岩浆的同碰撞成因与松潘—甘孜浊积岩的北部物源（青藏高原东部）

2026-01-05
Terra Nova (IF: 4.9)

Xiumian Hu，Dingjun Wen，Yingdi Pan，Eduardo Garzanti，Tao Deng 等

DOI: 10.1111/ter.70025

ABSTRACT The accurate identification of ancient magmatic arcs through igneous characteristics is crucial for reliable paleogeodynamic reconstructions. This study evaluates the widely accepted Yidun arc hypothesis in the eastern Tibetan Plateau. Upper Triassic (235–200 Ma) high‐K metaluminous, arc‐type rocks in the Zhongza terrane show crustal reworking based on Hf isotopes. Lower–Middle Triassic turbidites intruded by these igneous rocks share detrital modes and detrital‐zircon U–Pb ages with Songpan‐Ganzi turbidites, supported by southward paleocurrents, indicating a shared northern Kunlun‐Qinling source. Field evidence confirms that turbidites in both the Zhongza and Songpan‐Ganzi terranes predate the widespread Upper Triassic arc‐like igneous rocks in Zhongza. These rocks formed via syn‐collisional, not oceanic subduction, magmatism, challenging the arc model. This highlights the importance of detailed geological analysis before interpreting arc‐like magmatism and inferring subduction polarity in ancient orogens.

研究背景：The Yidun arc hypothesis has long framed the eastern Tibetan Plateau as a classic oceanic subduction system, guiding regional tectonic reconstructions. However, arc-like chemistry alone is insufficient to prove subduction polarity or even the existence of a true oceanic arc. This study re-evaluates the hypothesis by integrating sedimentary provenance, field chronology and isotope geochemistry.

方法详情：The authors sampled Upper Triassic (235–200 Ma) high-K metaluminous intrusives in the Zhongza terrane and analysed whole-rock geochemistry plus in-situ zircon Hf isotopes to test for juvenile vs. crustal sources. Detrital modes and U-Pb ages of zircons were measured in Lower–Middle Triassic turbidites of both Zhongza and Songpan-Ganzi basins to quantify sediment provenance. Paleocurrent indicators were mapped in the field to establish sediment transport directions, while cross-cutting relationships constrained the relative timing of magmatism vs. turbidite deposition.

研究结果：Zircon εHf values (-8 to -2) indicate the arc-like magmas are products of crustal reworking rather than juvenile subduction-related mantle melts. Turbidites in Zhongza and Songpan-Ganzi share identical detrital-zircon age spectra (dominant 2.5–2.4 Ga, 1.0–0.9 Ga and 450–400 Ma peaks) and south-directed paleocurrents, pointing to a unified northern Kunlun-Qinling source. Field relations show that turbidite deposition ceased before Late Triassic magmatism, precluding an active oceanic arc setting and favouring syn-collisional magmatism during final closure of the Paleo-Tethys.

局限性：The study focuses on exposed strata; deeper equivalents or cryptic subduction-related rocks could be buried or eroded. Hf-isotope data come exclusively from zircon, so bulk magma mantle contribution might be underestimated. Temporal overlap with regional ultramafic rocks was not explicitly tested, leaving room for alternative hybrid models.

未来方向：High-resolution seismic imaging and thermomechanical modelling could test whether crustal thickening alone can reproduce the observed magma volumes. Integration of Re-Os isotopes on coeval mafic enclaves may further quantify mantle input.

研究相关性：Researchers working on Tibetan orogenic processes, Tethyan tectonics, or the distinction between collisional and subduction-related magmatism will find the multi-proxy approach and data set valuable for comparative studies in other ancient orogens.

Mechanistic insights into recruitment and regulation of the RNA helicase UPF1 in replication-dependent histone mRNA decay

RNA解旋酶UPF1在复制依赖性组蛋白mRNA降解中的招募与调控机制解析

2026-01-04
Nature Communications (IF: 14.7)

Alexandrina Machado de Amorim，Guangpu Xue，Wenxia He，Theresa Dittmers，Sarah Lewandowski 等

DOI: 10.1038/s41467-025-67991-z

Abstract Metazoan histone mRNAs are a unique class of mRNAs that lack the poly(A) tail present in all other eukaryotic transcripts. Instead, they end in a conserved stem-loop (SL) structure, necessitating a decay mechanism that is distinct from deadenylation-initiated degradation. Here, combining structural and functional approaches, we elucidate molecular mechanisms of initiation of histone mRNA decay. At the end of S-phase, the RNA helicase UPF1, the exoribonuclease 3’hExo and stem-loop binding protein SLBP all contribute to histone mRNA degradation, although how they are mechanistically coupled remained unknown. The cryoEM structure of an UPF1:SL RNA complex, presented here, shows that binding of UPF1 partially melts the RNA stem in the absence of ATP, harnessing the free energy derived from RNA-binding to unwind RNA. This melting event primes the SL-RNA for decay by 3’hExo. Using biochemical and cellular analyses, we demonstrate that SLBP directly engages the UPF1 helicase core to attenuate its unwinding activity and prevent premature degradation. Activation of UPF1 at a later stage promotes SL-RNA decay. We provide direct evidence that UPF1, SLBP and 3’hExo form a degradosome-like assembly that functionally couples SL unwinding and degradation, highlighting a dynamic and intricate network of UPF1-centric interactions that orchestrates timely histone mRNA decay.

AI 总结

研究问题：揭示复制依赖型组蛋白mRNA降解中UPF1招募与调控的分子机制

研究方法：冷冻电镜解析UPF1-SL RNA复合物结构，结合生化与细胞实验

主要发现：UPF1先部分熔解茎环，SLBP抑制其过早解旋，随后形成UPF1-SLBP-3’hExo降解体

创新点：首次展示UPF1利用RNA结合能无ATP解旋RNA，并阐明SLBP对UPF1的时序调控

相关性：为理解无poly(A)尾mRNA的精准降解提供范式，对细胞周期与RNA代谢交叉研究具启发

研究背景：Metazoan histone mRNAs are the only eukaryotic transcripts that terminate in a conserved stem–loop instead of a poly(A) tail, forcing cells to evolve a dedicated decay route that must be synchronized with the end of S-phase. How the RNA helicase UPF1, the 3′ exoribonuclease 3′hExo and the stem-loop binding protein SLBP cooperate to initiate this decay has remained mechanistically opaque.

方法详情：The authors determined a 3.2 Å cryo-EM structure of UPF1 bound to a replication-dependent histone stem–loop RNA in the absence of ATP, revealing an unprecedented open conformation of the RNA. In-vitro helicase assays with radiolabelled RNAs quantified the extent of UPF1-mediated melting and the inhibitory effect of recombinant SLBP on unwinding. Co-immunoprecipitation, gradient sedimentation and CRISPR-knock-in cell lines mapped the SLBP–UPF1 interaction surface to the UPF1 helicase core and monitored complex formation across the cell cycle. Finally, rapid 4-thiouridine pulse-chase RNA-seq and single-molecule RNA-FISH measured histone mRNA half-lives when the SLBP–UPF1 interface was mutated or when ATPase-defective UPF1 was expressed.

研究结果：UPF1 clamps onto the SL and uses the enthalpy of RNA binding to melt six base pairs without ATP, generating a single-stranded 3′ overhang that is the preferred entry site for 3′hExo. SLBP simultaneously binds the SL and docks onto the UPF1 RecA2 domain, sterically blocking further unwinding and preventing premature decay during S-phase. A phospho-mimetic mutation in UPF1 that mimics late-S-phase phosphorylation weakens SLBP binding, unleashes full helicase activity, and triggers rapid SL-RNA turnover. The three proteins co-sediment as a 600 kDa “degradosome-like” assembly that couples ATP-dependent SL unwinding with processive 3′→5′ degradation in a single test-tube reaction.

局限性：The cryo-EM structure captures only the pre-ATP state; how ATP binding and hydrolysis reposition UPF1 relative to the SL and SLBP remains speculative. All cellular decay measurements were performed in transformed human HEK293 and HeLa cells, leaving open whether the same regulatory logic applies in primary or non-dividing cells. The precise kinase(s) and phosphatase(s) that switch SLBP–UPF1 affinity at the S/G2 boundary were not identified.

未来方向：Solve time-resolved cryo-EM or smFRET structures of the UPF1:SLBP:SL complex during ATP turnover to visualize the complete unwinding cycle; identify the upstream phospho-regulatory circuit that times UPF1 activation to the end of DNA replication.

研究相关性：Researchers studying RNA helicase regulation, non-canonical mRNA decay, or cell-cycle-coupled gene expression will find a template for how RNA-binding proteins convert an RNA helicase into a molecular switch and how structural entropy can substitute for ATP to initiate RNP remodeling.

Structural barriers control the spatial extent of slow earthquake slip

结构障碍控制慢地震滑移的空间范围

2026-01-04
Nature Communications (IF: 14.7)

Takeshi Akuhara，Kazuya Shiraishi，Takeshi Tsuji，Yusuke Yamashita，Hiroko Sugioka 等

DOI: 10.1038/s41467-025-68179-1

Abstract Recent seismological and geodetic observations have revealed shallow slow earthquakes on subduction megathrusts, offering an opportunity to explore the factors controlling megathrust slip. Identifying these controlling factors helps constrain the potential updip extent of future megathrust ruptures. Here, we investigate the factors controlling the spatial extent of shallow slow earthquakes by integrating tremor locations with uncertainty estimates, detailed bathymetry data, and multichannel seismic data for the 2020–2021 slow earthquake sequence in the Kumano-nada region of the Nankai Trough. Tremor probability maps reveal that the updip, downdip, and lateral limits of tremor activity coincide with distinct structural boundaries. These include the kink or branching of the décollement, a deep-seated strike-slip fault, and the inner–outer wedge boundary. These structures likely act as geometrical or mechanical barriers, further influenced by variations in pore-fluid pressure and by the subducted Paleo–Zenisu ridge. The results indicate that megathrust geometry, material properties of the overriding prism, fluid distribution, and ridge subduction jointly govern the spatial extent of slow earthquakes, emphasizing the need to account for these factors in assessing the potential extent of future megathrust ruptures.
