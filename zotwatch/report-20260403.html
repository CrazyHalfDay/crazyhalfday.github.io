Phengite-mediated fluorine and chlorine fluxes from subduction zones to the deep mantle

由金云母介导的从俯冲带到深部地幔的氟和氯通量

2026-04-01
Science Advances (IF: 11.7)

Yingzhuo Liu，Yu Wang，Chunfei Chen，Mingdi Gao，Yi-Gang Xu

DOI: 10.1126/sciadv.aee6110

The cycling of volatiles through subduction zones fundamentally shapes Earth’s chemical evolution, yet how fluorine (F) and chlorine (Cl) are processed in the deep mantle remains enigmatic. Here, we use high-pressure experiments (5 to 11 gigapascals and 850° to 1200°C) on altered oceanic crust analogs to track deep halogen behavior. We show that phengite remains stable to 11 gigapascals and 1050°C, efficiently transporting F and Cl to ~330-kilometer depth. Upon breakdown or melting, phengite strongly fractionates these halogens: Cl is released into fluids or melts, while F is sequestered in residual phases such as KMgF 3 . The Cl-rich fluids produced (9.6 to 19.9 wt % Cl) closely match high-density fluid inclusions in diamonds, implicating them as key agents of cratonic metasomatism. Our findings establish phengite as a critical carrier mediating postarc fluxes of F (1.7 × 10 12 to 2.6 × 10 12 grams per year) and Cl (0.52 × 10 12 to 1.1 × 10 12 grams per year), providing a major pathway for replenishing deep mantle halogen reservoirs.

研究背景：俯冲带是地球挥发分循环的核心通道，决定了卤素等元素如何在地表、地壳和地幔之间迁移，并深刻影响地球长期化学演化。然而，相比水和碳，氟（F）与氯（Cl）在深部俯冲过程中如何被保存、分异并输送到深部地幔，长期缺乏直接实验约束。该研究的动机在于识别俯冲洋壳中能够稳定携带卤素进入深部地幔的关键矿物相，并量化其对深部地幔卤素补给和克拉通交代作用的贡献。

方法详情：作者采用高压高温实验方法，使用蚀变洋壳（altered oceanic crust）模拟体系，在5至11 GPa、850至1200°C的条件下系统追踪F和Cl在俯冲环境中的分配行为，这一压力温度范围对应俯冲板片进入上地幔深部至约330公里深度的条件。实验重点考察了富钾白云母高压变种——phengite（多硅白云母/硅铁白云母）在不同温压条件下的稳定性，以及其在分解或发生部分熔融时卤素在矿物、熔体和流体之间的再分配。研究还识别了残留相中可能固定F的矿物，如KMgF3，并测定或估算了流体/熔体中的Cl富集程度。最后，作者将实验结果与天然金刚石中的高密度流体包裹体成分进行对比，并结合俯冲通量估算，推导phengite介导的深部F、Cl后弧输送通量。

研究结果：研究表明，phengite在高达11 GPa和1050°C的条件下仍可保持稳定，说明它能够在俯冲过程中高效携带F和Cl到约330公里深度，是深部卤素输运的重要载体。当phengite分解或体系发生熔融时，F和Cl表现出显著分异：Cl优先进入流体或熔体，而F则更倾向于保留在残留固相中，例如KMgF3等含氟相。实验产生的Cl富流体含有9.6至19.9 wt%的Cl，与金刚石高密度流体包裹体的成分高度相似，这支持了此类流体可能是克拉通地幔交代作用的重要介质。基于这些结果，作者进一步估算phengite控制的后弧深部通量约为F的1.7×10^12至2.6×10^12克/年、Cl的0.52×10^12至1.1×10^12克/年，表明该机制可能是补给深部地幔卤素储库的主要途径之一。

局限性：首先，该研究主要基于实验室模拟的蚀变洋壳类比体系，天然俯冲板片在成分、氧逸度、含水量和变质历史上的复杂性，可能导致实际卤素行为比实验结果更具空间和时间上的非均一性。其次，实验虽覆盖了关键的上地幔深部条件，但对更深部条件、不同俯冲热结构以及与其他含卤矿物共同作用的约束仍然有限。另一个潜在问题是全球通量估算依赖若干参数假设，例如俯冲输入组成和矿物稳定域范围，因此数值上仍存在一定不确定性。

未来方向：后续研究可进一步扩展到更高压温条件，以检验F和Cl在过渡带乃至更深地幔中的持续迁移与储存机制。与此同时，结合天然样品地球化学、流体包裹体分析和热力学建模，将有助于更精确评估不同类型俯冲带中的全球卤素循环通量。

研究相关性：这篇论文对研究俯冲带地球化学、挥发分循环、深部流体作用、地幔交代和克拉通演化的研究者都具有很高相关性。其价值在于，不仅识别了phengite这一关键卤素载体，还揭示了F与Cl在深部过程中的显著分异机制，为理解弧后挥发分输送、金刚石包裹体成因、深部地幔卤素储库形成以及俯冲板片对地幔化学改造的贡献提供了新的实验依据和定量框架。对于关注地球内部挥发分质量平衡、矿物-流体分配行为以及深部地球系统耦合过程的研究者而言，这项工作提供了可直接用于模型构建和天然样品解释的关键约束。

Mantle plume-lithosphere interaction: Evolution and resource environmental effects

地幔柱—岩石圈相互作用：演化及其资源环境效应

2026-03-26
Science China Earth Sciences (IF: 5.0)

Wei Leng，Shi Wang，Hao Liu

DOI: 10.1007/s11430-025-1842-0

Mantle plumes represent crucial carriers for the cross-layer transport of material and energy within Earth’s interior. Originating from the deep interior, they carry deep-seated materials upward to interact with the lithosphere, generating significant volcanic activity. Mantle plumes exhibit a characteristic head-tail morphology, where their thermal anomaly and carried chemical components (e.g., primitive mantle, subducted oceanic crust) significantly influence their morphology and the composition of related surface volcanic rocks. Upon interaction with the lithosphere, the plume head can trigger large-scale flood basalt eruptions and the formation of large igneous provinces (LIPs), while the plume tail gives rise to hotspot tracks, controlling their associated topography and heat flow distribution. These interaction processes are influenced by source composition, lithospheric thickness, mantle rheological structure, etc. The plume-lithosphere interaction has important resource and environmental implications: first, the released volatiles can trigger climate anomalies and even mass extinction events, second, it can form various mineral deposits. Future research should focus on whole-lifecycle modeling of the plume, integrating geochemical and geodynamical models, investigating plume activity within the supercontinent cycle framework, and simulating plume-related mineralization processes to advance our understanding of this key geodynamic process.

DOI: 10.1016/j.chemgeo.2026.123397

Molybdenum (Mo) isotopes exhibit significant fractionation during subduction zone processes, providing a powerful tool for tracing fluid activity. Here we report the Mo isotopic compositions of paired metamorphic rocks (eclogites and gneisses) and magmatic rocks (gabbroic diorites and monzodiorites) from the Dabie-Sulu continental collision zone. Collectively, the light Mo isotopic features of the metamorphic and magmatic rocks point to significant Mo isotopic fractionation during continental subduction. Subsequently, melting of this dehydrated crust produced melts that metasomatized the overlying mantle wedge, generating the source of low-δ 98 Mo magmatism in the continental collision zone. Therefore, the Mo isotope systematics of metamorphic and magmatic rocks provide a powerful means of revealing potential significant fluid activity in continental subduction zones.

Sulfur (S) is thought to degas deep from hydrous magmas (e.g., arc basalts), in contrast to water-poor magmas where S degasses at very shallow depths (e.g., Kīlauea, mid-ocean ridge basalts). The dilution effect impacts all volatile species, with its magnitude depending on the relative solubilities and concentrations of the different volatiles present.

Sedimentary Mn carbonate deposits (SMCDs) constitute the dominant global reservoir of Mn, a critical metal essential for modern technologies, yet their formation timing and links to environmental perturbations remain elusive owing to sparse datable minerals and coarse chronostratigraphic resolution. Here, we report astronomical cycle signals preserved in P (indicative of marine productivity) and (Zr+K)/Al (indicative of detrital flux and relative sea level) from drill core ZK2407 of the Xialei SMCD (∼143 Mt Mn), South China, which is embedded in Late Devonian platform/slope facies strata of the Youjiang Basin. Leveraging the 405 kyr eccentricity cycle and conodont biostratigraphy, the astronomical timescale for the Xialei SMCD was established. The deposition of ore layers I and II+III in the Xialei SMCD took ∼130 and ∼550 kyr, respectively. Ore layer I aligns precisely with the Late Famennian Annulata Event (∼363.4 Ma), while ore layer II+III coincides with the Dasberg Event (∼361.9 Ma). As both mass extinctions were volcanically driven, these temporal couplings suggest large volcanic eruptions as the primary forcing for Mn metallogeny. Massive CO2 emissions from large volcanism induced global warming and sea-level rise, fostering redox-stratified oceanic conditions conducive to SMCD formation. Mn concentrations covary positively in-phase with P and sea-level, indicating transgression-mediated co-transport of anoxic deep-sea hydrothermal Mn(II) and nutrients to the oxic shallow sea, where Mn(III/IV) oxides precipitated. The resulting heightened productivity drove organic matter burial, which subsequently facilitated the reductive transformation of these oxides into Mn carbonates during early diagenesis. Orbital forcing modulated the rhythm of Mn enrichment on astronomical timescales by periodically driving paleoenvironmental variations. This work delineates a temporal framework for the formation of SMCDs and demonstrates that the metallogeny of SMCDs resulted from paleoceanographic changes induced by large volcanism and orbital dynamics.
