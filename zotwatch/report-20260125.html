Carbon cycling during crustal magmatism: Constraints from Fe-Mg isotopes of Early Cretaceous adakitic rocks in Liaodong Peninsula, NE China

地壳岩浆作用过程中的碳循环：来自中国东北辽东半岛早白垩世埃达克质岩铁镁同位素的制约

2026-01-23
Geological Society of America Bulletin (IF: 3.9)

Yikang Quan，Jiaru Sheng，Haochen Duan，Wei Zhang，Yan Jing 等

DOI: 10.1130/b38747.1

Carbon cycling mediated by magmatism is fundamental to planetary habitability, yet the role of crustal magmatism, particularly adakitic rocks, in tracing deep carbon transfer remains poorly constrained. Here, we employ coupled Fe-Mg isotopes in Early Cretaceous low-Mg (LMA) and high-Mg adakitic rocks (HMA) from northeastern China to identify the geochemical fingerprint of carbon cycling within crustal magmatism. The LMA exhibit high δ56Fe (0.14‰ to 0.23‰) and low δ26Mg (−0.40‰ to −0.28‰), whereas HMA display low δ56Fe (0.05‰ to 0.15‰) and high δ26Mg (−0.31‰ to −0.21‰). Partial melting modeling and major-element variations indicate that they originated from a carbonated eclogite source. The widespread distribution of Mesozoic adakitic rocks across northeastern China demonstrates that carbonated eclogite is a critical carbon reservoir. Temporal correlations between regional Mg isotope compilations and subducted carbon fluxes reveal that slab subduction (Paleo-Pacific plate in Mesozoic; Pacific plate in Cenozoic) facilitated carbon recycling. Our results establish adakitic rocks as robust tracers of deep carbon cycling and demonstrate that plate-tectonic processes have governed this transfer over millions of years, advancing understanding of Earth’s deep carbon cycle and its planetary habitability implications.

Seismic Attenuation Tomography in Central Myanmar and Its Implications on Continental Subduction and Arc Magmatism

缅甸中部的地震衰减层析成像及其对大陆俯冲和弧岩浆作用的启示

2026-01-23
Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth (IF: 3.9)

Yilin Feng，Yinshuang Ai，Luca De Siena，Yumei He，Mingming Jiang 等

DOI: 10.1029/2025jb032147

Abstract Myanmar is located on the eastern margin of the India‐Eurasia collision zone, where the Indian sub‐continent is subducting beneath the Burma microplate. Magmatic processes during subduction and collision in orogenic belts are significant and well‐studied for oceanic subduction; however, the magmatism associated with continental subduction remains poorly understood. Seismic attenuation is highly sensitive to changes in lithospheric thermodynamics and fluid content. Understanding arc volcanism is vital for comprehending a key manifestation of subduction‐related processes. However, there is still no high‐resolution 3D attenuation model for this region. Here, we use the coda‐normalized method to image the lithospheric‐scale 3D attenuation structure in the Indo‐Burma subduction zone. Our results reveal high attenuation in major sedimentary basins. The prominent high‐attenuation anomalies in the mid‐to‐lower crust of the Indo‐Burma Ranges (IBR) may represent thick, fluid‐rich sediments scraped off from the subducting Indian Plate and accumulated beneath the IBR. Low‐attenuation anomalies at depths of 30–50 km beneath the Monywa volcano are a clear signature of a cooled mantle wedge, which currently overlies strong attenuation anomalies deeper than 50 km, likely associated with the upwelling of hot asthenospheric material. Compared to oceanic subduction systems, the insufficient water content of the continental subduction plate, coupled with the compressional regime induced by oblique subduction, leads to weak attenuation within the mantle wedge.

创新点：明确了无震滑动在连接不同类型俯冲带地震事件中的关键触发机制。

研究背景：巽他海沟（Sunda Trench）是全球地震和海啸灾害最严重的区域之一。本研究旨在探讨先前发生的巨型逆冲地震（megathrust event）之后的无震滑移（aseismic slip）过程，如何改变断层应力状态，进而促进后续海啸地震（tsunamigenic earthquake）的发生，以揭示深部与浅部断层活动之间的动力学联系。

方法详情：研究团队综合利用了高精度的地表大地测量数据（如GPS/GNSS）和地震波形记录，通过运动学滑移反演模型重建了断层破裂过程。同时，采用库仑应力变化（Coulomb stress change）计算方法，模拟了先前地震及其后的无震后滑（afterslip）对浅部海沟区域的应力加载效应。

研究结果：研究发现，先前深部大地震后的无震滑移显著增加了浅部断层闭锁区的剪切应力，这种持续的应力传递有效地促进了后续海啸地震的触发。结果表明，无震滑移是连接深部震源破裂与浅部海啸灾害发生的关键驱动机制，解释了海啸地震在时间和空间上的分布特征。

局限性：由于缺乏近场的海底大地测量观测数据，对海沟附近的浅部滑移分布和闭锁状态的约束可能存在一定的不确定性。此外，模型中对断层摩擦属性和地壳流变学参数的假设可能简化了复杂的实际地质环境。

未来方向：未来的研究应致力于部署海底大地测量网络（seafloor geodesy）以获取更直接的浅部断层监测数据。同时，建议将此应力触发模型应用于全球其他类似的俯冲带区域，以评估潜在的海啸地震风险并改进早期预警系统。

研究相关性：这篇论文对于从事地震物理学、大地测量学以及海啸灾害评估的研究者具有高度相关性。它不仅提供了关于断层分段相互作用的新见解，还强调了监测无震滑移对于理解地震触发机制和评估海啸潜力的重要性。
