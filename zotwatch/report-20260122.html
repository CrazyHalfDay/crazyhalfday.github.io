Seamount subduction and its role in triggering magmatic flare-ups: Evidence from the Late Devonian Northern Yili Arc

海山俯冲及其诱发岩浆爆发的作用：来自晚泥盆世北伊犁弧的证据

2026-01-21
Geological Society of America Bulletin (IF: 3.9)

Hao Xing，Junling Pei，Xiaobo Zhao，Guangxian Liu，Jinfeng Wen 等

DOI: 10.1130/b38166.1

Magmatic flare-ups are widely recognized as pivotal features of continental arc magmatism, yet their driving mechanisms remain poorly understood. In this study, we present new radiometric ages and geochemical data for normal calc-alkaline, high-magnesian, and Nb-enriched arc magmatic rocks from the Northern Yili Arc (Western Tianshan, China), which coincide with the Late Devonian magmatic flare-up in the region. The normal arc magmatic rocks likely originated from partial melting of a slab fluid-metasomatized mantle wedge, possibly accompanied by fractional crystallization and crustal assimilation processes. In contrast, the high-magnesian andesites (HMAs) and Nb-enriched basalts (NEBs) were derived from sediment melt and slab melt-metasomatized mantle sources, respectively. The systematic decoupling of Nd-Hf isotopes in contemporaneous mafic-intermediate lavas further indicates mantle source heterogeneity. The clustered eruption of HMAs and NEBs, alongside enriched continental signatures in their mantle sources, implies intensified slab-mantle interactions. We attribute this to enhanced interaction efficiency driven by a unique geodynamic process. We propose that the most plausible mechanism for the Late Devonian−Early Carboniferous magmatic fluctuations involves seamount/oceanic plateau subduction followed by slab retreat or rollback. The Late Devonian seamount subduction scenario provides new insights into the geodynamic evolution of the North Tianshan Ocean, the distribution of Au-Cu deposits, and the polarity of subduction in ancient oceanic plates within the Yili microcontinent.

方法详情：研究团队对该地区的正常钙碱性岩石、高镁安山岩（HMAs）和富铌玄武岩（NEBs）进行了新的辐射定年和详细的地球化学数据分析。通过综合分析同位素（特别是Nd-Hf同位素解耦现象）和微量元素特征，研究者反演了岩浆源区的性质、部分熔融过程以及地壳混染程度。

Calbuco and Osorno volcanoes (Chile) tap into mantle sources that only differ by their water content

智利Calbuco和Osorno火山源自仅含水量不同的地幔源区

2026-01-20
Contributions to Mineralogy and Petrology (IF: 3.5)

Jacqueline Vander Auwera，Tonin Bechon，Salvatrice Montalbano，Bernard Charlier，Olivier Bolle 等

DOI: 10.1007/s00410-025-02282-w

The Osorno and Calbuco stratovolcanoes are situated just 27&nbsp;km apart along the volcanic arc front of the Central Southern Volcanic Zone (Chile), yet they exhibit significantly different characteristics. Notably, Calbuco intermediate to andesitic lavas contain amphibole, which is absent in Osorno, and has a much higher explosivity (up to VEI = 4 to 5). Their olivine-hosted melt inclusions (MI) display similar major elements differentiation trends. Incompatible trace element ratios overlap in both volcanoes and therefore do not correlate with the H2O and Cl contents. Indeed, MIs data and petrological hygrometers indicate higher volatiles content at Calbuco (~ 3 wt% @ 51.3 wt% SiO2, 951–3232 ppm) compared to Osorno (~ 1 wt% @ 50.5 wt% SiO2, 521–1091 ppm). Because the Cpx-only geobarometers and geophysical data both place the Osorno and Calbuco main magma reservoirs at the same depth, their differing volatile contents are interpreted as inherited from the mantle melts. Their calculated primary magmas result from similar to identical partial melting fractions of mantle sources that differ only in their H2O content. The mantle source beneath Calbuco partially melted at a lower temperature, possibly because of the slightly more trench ward position of the volcano, which projects vertically into a cooler part of the mantle wedge “nose”. The lack of correlation between incompatible elements and H2O content is interpreted as resulting from evidence for a decoupling between H2O, transported mainly in a solute-poor fluid, and incompatible elements, which are delivered by melts derived from the subducting oceanic lithosphere. The variability of the mantle wedge H2O concentration and the uncorrelated content of slab tracers and H2O have also been observed at a global scale.

研究问题：探讨相距仅27公里的Calbuco和Osorno火山为何表现出截然不同的喷发特征和矿物组成。

研究方法：通过分析橄榄石熔体包裹体的主微量元素和挥发分，结合岩石湿度计和地质压力计进行对比研究。

主要发现：两者岩浆源区深度相同，主要差异在于Calbuco源区含水量更高且熔融温度更低。

创新点：提出了水（流体运输）与不相容元素（熔体运输）解耦的机制，解释了两者含量无相关性的现象。

相关性：揭示了地幔楔含水量的变化及挥发分与示踪剂的解耦，有助于理解弧岩浆作用和火山爆发力。

研究背景：Osorno 和 Calbuco 火山位于智利中南部火山带前缘，相距仅 27 公里，但表现出显著不同的特征，其中 Calbuco 含有角闪石且爆发性更强（VEI 高达 4-5）。本研究旨在探究这两座地理位置极近但岩石学特征和喷发行为迥异的火山，其背后的岩浆成因及地幔源区机制的差异。

方法详情：研究人员分析了橄榄石中的熔体包裹体（MI），对比了主量元素分异趋势和不相容微量元素比值。利用岩石学湿度计测定挥发分（H2O 和 Cl）含量，结合单斜辉石（Cpx-only）地质压力计及地球物理数据确定岩浆储库深度，并通过反算模拟原始岩浆的性质以推断其地幔源区的温度和部分熔融程度。

研究结果：尽管两座火山的微量元素比值重叠且岩浆储库深度相同，但 Calbuco 的挥发分含量（~3 wt%）显著高于 Osorno（~1 wt%）。这种差异被证实继承自地幔源区：Calbuco 下方的地幔源区因位置稍靠海沟一侧，处于地幔楔“鼻”的较冷区域，导致部分熔融温度较低且含水量较高。研究还揭示了 H2O（主要由贫溶质流体输送）与不相容元素（由板片来源的熔体输送）在俯冲带岩浆形成过程中的解耦现象。

局限性：研究结论高度依赖于熔体包裹体数据的保真度，包裹体在捕获后可能发生的组分调整（如 H2O 扩散或后其结晶）可能影响定量结果的精确性。此外，关于地幔楔精细热结构和流体/熔体具体输运机制的解释主要基于地球化学反演，可能存在模型依赖性的不确定性。

未来方向：未来的研究可以进一步在其他俯冲带区域验证流体与熔体解耦的普遍性，并结合高分辨率地球物理成像技术更精确地约束地幔楔的热结构与流体分布。

研究相关性：这篇论文对于研究俯冲带岩浆作用、挥发分循环以及火山灾害评估的研究者具有重要参考价值，特别是它提供了一个关于同弧相邻火山如何因地幔源区含水量微小差异而导致巨大喷发行为差异的经典案例。

Electrical Conductivity of Carbonatite Melts to 20 GPa: Constraints on Partial Melting Atop the 410‐km Discontinuity and in the Lower Mantle Transition Zone

20 GPa以内碳酸盐熔体的电导率：对410公里间断面顶部及地幔过渡带下部部分熔融的约束

2026-01-20
Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth (IF: 3.9)

Bin Zhao，Jintao Zhu，Qi Chen，Takashi Yoshino

DOI: 10.1029/2025jb033390

Abstract Deep‐origin carbonatite melts are considered to be the products of partial‐melting of the oceanic crust in the subduction zones. In this study, we conducted electrical conductivity (EC) measurements on two samples, the composition of which resemble the partial‐melting products atop the 410‐km discontinuity and in the lower part of the transition zone. The EC of carbonatite melts was investigated using impedance spectroscopy combined with a multi‐anvil press up to 20 GPa. Pressure has a great effect on the EC of the carbonatite melts. While the EC dropped overall by 0.6 log unit from 3 to 20 GPa for varying compositions, the pressure effect becomes weaker above 10 GPa. The Hashin‐Shtrikman mixing model indicates that melt fraction of 0–0.3 vol% is necessary to account for the EC atop the 410‐km discontinuity beneath NE China, north Philippine Sea, north Pacific, and Australian craton. However, this value soars to 1–4.5 vol% for the lower part of the transition zone in the same regions, and further increases to 3.7–7.3 vol% for cold subduction regions if the slab surface temperature is 300 K lower. The difference in the needed melt fraction at different depths implies that the magnitude of partial melting is much larger in the lower part of the mantle transition zone, and it is thus likely to be the main barrier to the recycled carbonates towards the deep interior.

AI 总结

研究问题：研究高压下碳酸盐熔体电导率，以约束地幔转换带顶部及下部的部分熔融程度。

研究方法：利用多砧压机结合阻抗谱技术，在最高20 GPa压力下测量了两种模拟熔体的电导率。

主要发现：压力显著降低电导率；模型显示地幔转换带下部所需熔体分数远高于410-km顶部。

创新点：将测量压力扩展至20 GPa，揭示地幔转换带下部是阻碍循环碳酸盐进入深部的主要屏障。

相关性：为解释地幔高导异常及理解深部碳循环机制提供了关键的高压实验数据约束。

研究背景：深源碳酸盐熔体被认为是俯冲带中洋壳部分熔融的产物，对理解深部碳循环至关重要。本研究旨在通过实验测定碳酸盐熔体在高温高压下的电导率，以约束410公里不连续面顶部和下地幔过渡带的部分熔融程度及熔体分布。

方法详情：研究人员制备了两种模拟410公里不连续面顶部和下地幔过渡带部分熔融产物成分的样品。利用多砧压机结合阻抗谱技术，在高达20 GPa的压力范围内测量了这些碳酸盐熔体的电导率。随后，应用Hashin-Shtrikman混合模型，根据实验数据估算了不同地质构造背景下所需的熔体体积分数。

研究结果：研究发现压力显著影响碳酸盐熔体的电导率，从3 GPa到20 GPa电导率整体下降约0.6个对数单位，但在10 GPa后压力效应减弱。模型显示，解释中国东北等地410公里处的电导率仅需0-0.3 vol%的熔体，而下地幔过渡带则需1-4.5 vol%（冷俯冲带高达3.7-7.3 vol%）。这表明下地幔过渡带的部分熔融程度远高于上部，可能是阻止循环碳酸盐进入地球深部的主要屏障。

局限性：实验虽然覆盖了高达20 GPa的压力，但实验室合成样品可能无法完全涵盖自然界中复杂的微量元素和挥发分变化。此外，利用混合模型将实验数据外推解释大尺度地球物理观测数据时，可能受到模型假设和地幔温度估算不确定性的影响。

未来方向：未来的研究可以进一步探索更高压力（如下地幔环境）下的熔体电导率特性，或者结合地震波速度测量进行多物理场联合反演，以更精确地约束地幔深部的物质组成。

研究相关性：这篇论文对于研究地球深部碳循环、地幔过渡带结构以及利用大地电磁数据解释地幔异常的研究者具有高度相关性，特别是对于关注俯冲带物质再循环机制的地球物理学家和实验岩石学家。

Wafer-scale high-κ HfO2 dielectric films with sub-5-Å equivalent oxide thickness for 2D MoS2 transistors

Nature Communications (IF: 14.7)

DOI: 10.1038/s41467-026-68584-0

High-κ gate dielectrics are indispensable in modern transistor technology and play a pivotal role in efficient capacitive gating and suppression of leakage currents. However, the realization of industry-compatible high-κ gate dielectrics at a sub-5-Å equivalent oxide thickness (EOT) remains challenging. Here we report the realization of 1.3-nm thick hafnium oxide (HfO2) dielectrics via an industry-compatible multiple oxidation atomic layer deposition process at 200 °C. A low EOT down to 2.5 Å is demonstrated for 1.3-nm thick HfO2 dielectrics on metal gates with a low leakage current of 10−6 A/cm2 and a robust breakdown electric field of ~22.3 MV/cm. Remarkably, such low EOT high-κ/metal gates can be directly implanted into emerging two-dimensional (2D) transistors and low-power logic circuits on 8-inch wafer scale to showcase their potentials. The as-fabricated molybdenum disulfide (MoS2) transistors exhibit a large on-state current density of 260 µA/µm at source-drain bias of 0.5 V, a high on/off ratio of 108, an average subthreshold slope (SS) of 75 mV/dec, and small capacitance equivalent thickness (CET) values of 0.34 nm for gate-first transistors and 0.50 nm for gate-last transistors. Our ultra-scaled dielectrics hold significant promise for advanced semiconductor fabrication processes towards the angstrom era. The integration of high-κ dielectrics with low equivalent oxide thickness (EOT) is crucial for the development of 2D transistors. Here, the authors report the low-temperature fabrication of wafer-scale HfO2 dielectric films with sub-5-Å EOT and their application for the realization of high-performance 2D MoS2 transistors and circuits.
